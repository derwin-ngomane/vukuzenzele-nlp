
Vuk’uzenzeleIthemba Elitjha leSewula Afrika IHlelo leYES limzamosisungulo kaMengameli u-Ramaphosa anqophe ngawo ukuvulela ilutjha isango lamathuba wemisebenzi. Dale HesEsikhathini esi mnyaka esidluleko, uMengameli u-Cyril Ramaphosa watjala ithe mba elitjha ngeSewula  Afrika. Ngemva kokube kwa esitulweni, uMengame li wethula iKulumo yobu Jamo beNarha (i-SoNA) ekarisako eyavuselela ithe mba, ithabo nokuzithemba eengidini zabantu ezazi yibukele ngelizweni ma zombe.
Kwaba khona ukuthaka sa okudephileko ngeKu lumo yobuJamo beNar ha yomnyakawee-2018 (i-SoNA). Kubeyinto ehle u kwethulwa kwekulumo le ekhambisana nokugidi ngwa kweKhulu lemiNya ka yokuBelethwa kuka Mandela: akhenge selokhu kwafika intando yenengi uMengameli weSewula Afrika azizwele umthwa lo osemahlombakhe nje ngawo lo. Kodwana ikulu mo kaMengameli u-Rama phosa yathinta zoke ii nhlokwana ezazilinde lwe mphakathi weSewula  Afrika. Yathinta izinto ezibu rhaba eziqalene nelizwe; yatjhukumisa nepengu enobuhlakani begodu ya tjengisa ukuzibophelela ekwakheni iSewula Afrika engcono.
Imikhakha Enqotjhwe yi-SoNA yomnyaka wee-2018Njengawo woke ama SoNA ethulwako ngapha mbilini, iindaba zokuvulwa kwamathuba wemisebenzi, ukukhulisa umnotho, uku rhobhisa umtlhago, zefundo, zamaphilo, ukuvuselelwa kobunini benarha, ukulinga na ngokobulili nokulwisana nobulelesi nekohlakalo zivele khulu ku-ajenda ye-SoNA yomnyaka wee-2018.
UMengameli uRama phosa wathi isikhathesi singesokubelethwa kabutjha ngokupheleleko kweSewula Afrika. Isikhathi esitlhoga izinga elitjha lokurarulula imiraro. “Sinikelwe umsebenzi wokwakha isitjhaba esitjha, ngomnqopho wokuqalana nesitjhijilo sokudinywa ubu lungiswa esikhathini sanga phambilini nokungalingani ngokomnotho kwagadesi.
Sikhonjelwe ukwenza ama tjhuguluko la ngaphasi kobu jamo obubudisi.”Ukuvulwa Kwamathuba Wemisebenzi  ummongo wokuphelisa u mtlhago, ukuvulwa kwama thuba wemisebenzi kunge linye lamaphuzu wokuthoma elivezwe ngu-Ramaphosa ekulumenakhe.
“Ummongo we-ajenda yethu yelizweloke yomnyaka  wee-2018 kuvulwa kwama thuba wemisebenzi, khu lukhulu yelutjha,” kwatjho u-Ramaphosa.
Njengombana likhula izinga lokutlhayela kwemisebenzi eSewula Afrika, uMengameli wagandelela isidingo esirha bako sokuvula amathuba wemisebenzi ngokutha tha amagadango athileko.
Semukelwe ngezandla ezi futhumeleko isimemezelo sikaMengameli seMbizo yezemiSebenzi, ezokufaka hlangana imikhulumiswano hlangana neminyango  yombuso namakhamphani wangeqadi, abosomabhizinisi neenhlangano ezijamele u mphakathi.
IMbizo ebanjwe ekuthome ni kukaSewula le, ibe lisizo ekutlameni umhlahlandlela wokuvulwa kwemisebenzi.
Umtlamo wesivumelwano owatlikitlwa emhlanganweni uhlathulule amano woku ngenelela angaphezulu kwa ma-70, hlangana nokhunye, ukutjheja ngcono iindingo zomnotho wangekhaya, ukukhulisa nokunabisa imikhiqizo ethunyelwa nga phandle kwemikhawulo, ukuvulela ilutjha imisebenzi nokuthuthukisa imikhakha efana newezokulima, imi khiqizo, zeemayini nomnotho oza ngokusebenza ngeenzibi.
Ilutjha eliqalene nokutlhaye la kwemisebenzi namkha nomtlhago wokungasebenzi, liyaziswa bona uMengameli u-Ramaphosa utjheje qangi amahlelo azokusiza ilu tjha. IPhiko lezokuQatjhwa kweLutjha (i-YES) lahlonywa eenyangeni ezimbili ngemva kwe-SoNA yomnyaka wee2018, elizokunikela amano wokobana amabhizinisi aqatjhe ilutjha ngamahlelo wokubandulelwa emsebe nzini. Ekupheleni komnyaka wee-2018, amakhamphani ama-226 besele atlikitle isivumelwano sokusebenzi sa ihlelo le-YES, kanti ilutjha elizii-5 500 besele lifakwe emisebenzini.
Ukusimamisa UmnothoUmnotho weSewula gade ukhula kabuthaka begodu waqalana nokufadalala eku thomeni komnyaka wee-2018.
Ithemba labasisimali gade liphasi khulu kanti nemikha kha yezomnotho enekghono eliphakemeko beyingafumani ukusekelwa okutlhogekako ekufakeni isandla kezo mnotho.
Ngokukhambisana ne siqubulo esithi “isikhathi sethemba elitjha”, uMenga meli u-Ramaphosa wathoma ngejima lokukhanga abasi simali. Wahloma isiqhema seenthunywa ezine  ezikhe thekileko ukobana zizoku hlanganyela naye ekha mbeni lephasi loke eli nqophe ukudosa i-R1.2 yamathriliyoni  wamaranda wesisomali phakathi kwa nje nomnyaka wee-2023.
UMengameli u-Ramaphosa wabuye wakhambela iphasi ngomnqopho wokuyoku vuselela ithemba lamazwe wangaphandle lokusisa eSe wula Afrika.
Ijimeli lafikelela ekuba njweni kweKhonferensi yama Siso yeSewula Afrika, lapho uMengameli amemezela
amabhiliyoni ama-R290 wa masiso athenjisiweko woku ngezelela kumabhiliyoni  ama-R400 athenjiswe  mamazwe avakatjhela ekhe thwapha ngesikhathi sejima lokufuna amasiso. Kulinga niselwa bonyana amasiso la azakungezelela ngamabhili yoni ama-R330 engenisweni lomKhiqizo oPheleleko wangeKhaya (i-GDP) we Sewula Afrika ngomnyaka wee-2024, kuvulwe imisebenzi  ebalelwa eenkulungwaneni ezima-275 (275 000)  ngomnya ka.
Iqhinga likaMengameli u-Ramaphosa lokudzimele lisa nokuvuselela umnotho libukwe njengepengu eqa kathekileko ekuqalaneni neenqabo zomnotho weSewu la Afrika. Amatjhuguluko la amenyezelwe ngoKhukhula mungu athembisa ukurhatji swa kokusetjenziswa kwema li yokulungisa umthangala sisekelo, amatjhuguluko ke zokuthintana nemikhakheni yeemayini, ukuhlanganyelwa kwamaphrojekthi minyango yombuso namakhamphani azijameleko nehlelo lokwenza lula ukufumaneka kwama Visa  ngokuvulela amakha mphani wangaphandle afuna ukusebenza ekhethwapha.
Ukuruthulwa Komra bhu Wekohlakalo Noku qiniswa Kokuphatha  UMengameli utjengise uku zibophelela kwakhe okukhu lu ngokuruthulwa komrabhu wekohlakalo kurhulumende nemabubulweni wo mbuso.  Ikomitjhini ephenya ngokugolwa kombuso na mkha ngokuthathwa kwe lawulo lombuso yahlonywa msinyana ngemva kokube kwa kwakhe esitulwe ni,kunye nekomitjhini ephe nya i-Eskom nePhiko eliBu thelela umThelo leSewula Africa (i-SARS).
Ukutjhugululwa kwabarho li emabubulweni wombuso kwaba ngelinye itshwayo lokuzimisela kukaMenga meli epini yokulwisana nekohlakalo nokuqinisa zokuphatha.
