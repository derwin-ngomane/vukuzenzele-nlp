Distributed by: Printed by:NGOKUMISELWA kwenkqubo yobuchwepheshe bekhompyutha ebizwa ie-Recruitment  yokufaka isicelo somsebenzi ngekhompyutha, urhulumente ubonakalise ukuzinikela kwakhe ekuphuculeni ubomi bolutsha nabanemfundo enezidanga.
Abantu abafuna imi sebenzi kungeku dala baza kukwazi ukufaka izicelo zemisebe nzi karhulumente ngoku ngokusebenzisa inkqubo ye-e-Recruitment  esungu lwe nguMphathiswa weSebe lezabaSebenzi bakaRhulu mente noLawulo u-Ayanda Dlodlo.
UMphathiswa uthe  inkqubo yee-Recruitment iqaliswa kwiSebe lezabaSe benzi bakaRhulumente noLawulo (i-DPSA) kanti kungekudala iza kungena kwiinkonzo zikarhulumente ziphelele.
“Inkqubo yee-Recruitment  iyinxenye yenkqubo karhu lumente ebanzi eyenzelwe ukuba ingabinabugocigoci futhi elula ebantwini, ukuba bafake izicelo zemisebenzi kwiinkonzo zikarhulu mente, ingakumbi kulutsha.“Le nkqubo iza kuquka nokuhlaziywa kwemimise lo emalunga nayo yonke imisebenzi ekwinqanaba lokuqala kuyo yonke imi sebenzi ekwiinkonzo zika rhulumente ukuze kuvume leke ukuqeshwa kwabantu abatsha abafuna umsebenzi bengenawo amava kwimise benzi ekwinqanaba lokuqa la,” utshilo uMphathiswa uDlodlo.
Uthe akunakuphikiswa ukuba iinkqubo zangoku zokugaya abasebenzi zino bugocigoci kwabo bafuna imisebenzi kwaye amasebe karhulumente awaka hambelani okanye awaka phuhli ngokweenguquko zophuhliso lobuchwephe tshe lwala maxesha.
“Kwabo bangabafu ni-msebenzi, inkqubo entsha yee-Recruitment  iza kuba luncedo njengoko isekwe kuthungelwano lwewebhu kwaye iyafike leleka kwiindawo ezahlu ka-hlukeneyo ukwenzela bonke abafuna imisebenzi bafake izicelo kuvimba weenkcukacha ophantsi kolawulo oluyintloko,”u tshilo.
UMphathiswa uthe le nkqubo iza kwenza ukuba abafaki-zicelo bakwazi ukuqhagamshela iinkcazelo zabo zobomi (ii-CV) kunye namaxwebhu angamabaka la wabo ezemfundo kanye kwaye le nkqubo  ibanceda ekubeni bangachithi mali yokwenza iikopi ngeekopi qho befaka izicelo.
“Kubalulekile ukugxinini sa ukuba izicelo zemisebenzi kwiiNkonzo zikaRhulu mente ziya kwamkeleka naxa kusetyenziswe iifo mu eziphathekayo zeZ83 ukuze kubonelelwe nabo bangakwazi kufikelela kwi-intanethi kanti ziya kuphinda zibe khona na kwi-intanethi ngokolungise lelo lwekhompyutha ukuze ikhawuleziswe kwaye ibe lula inkqubo yokufakwa kwezicelo kwabo bane-inta nethi,” utshilo.
UMphathiswa uthe uncedo olufunyanwa ngamasebe karhulumente kukuba inkqubo le iwavumela ukuba afake izaziso zezi thuba zemisebenzi ekho mpyutheni ukuze ke ngoko kunciphe iindleko zenkqubo yangoku yokupapasha iza ziso zezithuba zemisebenzi kumaphephandaba.Le nkqubo yobuchule obu tsha ingumzamo wentsebe nziswano phakathi kweSebe leeNkonzo zikaRhulumente noLawulo kunye neSebe laseGauteng lee-Gove rnment.
