Vuk’uzenzeleAll government departments and its components have been directed to stop the practice of requiring certified copies of documents not older than three months to accompany applications for employment.
This decision follows complaints raised with President Cyril Ramaphosa on social media and through other channels about the requirements for compliance with public sector job applications.
Departments have been ad-vised to at least accept certified copies of documents submitted with an application for employment that are up to six months old, in those cases where the document certified does not have an expiry date that falls within the six-month period, the Presidency said.
 “Government has recognised that the practice to date impacts negatively on job seekers in the current economic climate in South Africa, and that there is a need to reduce the burden on job seekers to submit with each separate job application certified copies of supporting documents (such as academic qualifications, which are not older than three months),” the Presidency said in a statement.
The Presidency said this change will also reduce the workload that the certification of documents creates for the South African Police Service personnel, who will now be able to devote more time to their core function of ensuring that South Africans are and feel safe.SAnews.gov.za 

DIKGANGKAKARETSOGo tswa mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo semolaoDale HesFa e le gore le lekile ka moo le ka kgonang go baakanya lenyalo la lona mme go retetse, go tswa mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo e ka nna selo se se molemo se bobedi ba lona le ka itirelang sone. Go tswa mo lenyalong ke kgato e e boima e e ngamolang tlhogo mo malapeng a le mantsi mme e bile gape dithulaganyo tsa molao tsa kgato eno di ka go tlhakatlhakanya tlhogo.
Go tswa mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo ke fa kgotlatshekelo e fedisa lenyalo semmuso. Go na le mefuta e le mebedi ya go tswa mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo: Go tswa mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo ntle le go ganediwa – mo batho ka bobedi ba dumelanang gore ba tshwanetse go itokolola mo lenyalong la bona; go na gape le wa Go tswa mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo mme yo mongwe wa lona a sa dumelane le kakanyo eo – ke fa batho ba ba leng mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo ba sa dumelane go itokolola mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo kgotsa ba sa dumelane le ditlamorago tsa yona.
Josephine Peta, Motlhankedi wa Tsamaiso ya tsa Molao yo Mogolwane kwa Lefapheng la Bosiamisi le Tlhabololo ya Molaotheo mo Katorong ya Babueledi ba Merero ya Malapa, o tlhalositse gore thulaganyo ya go kgaogana mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo e simolola gangwe fela fa kgotlatsekelo e go rebo letse taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo.
“Motho yo a batlang go tswa mo lenyalong a ka kopa taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo mabapi le go lokololwa mo lenyalong kwa kgotlatshekelokgolo kgotsa kwa kgotlatshekelo ya kgaolo, kgotsa a ka ikopanya le mmueledi go mo thusa ka thulaganyo eo. Mokapelo yo a tlhatlhelang kgetse eno ya thu laganyo ya go itokolola mo lenyalong o bediwa mongongoregi mme mokapelo yo mongwe yo kgetse e bulwang kgatlhanong le ene o bediwa  mosekisiwa,” Peta o tlhalositse jalo.
Peta o tlhalositse gore taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo mabapi le go lokololwa mo lenyalong e tshwanetse e:
• tlhalose gore ga go sa na se sengwe gape se se ka diriwang go tsosolosa kamano ya bona,• tlhagise kgato e e ka tsewang go kgaogana dithoto kgotsa dikgato tse di gapeletsang tiragatso ya dilo tse di utlwanetsweng fa go ne go tsenwa mo lenyalong,  le • tlhalose dithulaganyo tse di tla diriwang mabapi le bana ba baratani bano, tse di jaaka gore ke mang yo a tla tsayang bana.
“Taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo e tshwanetse e to tobatse gape le gore  mosekisiwa  o neilwe matsatsi a le 10 – fa e le gore o nna le mongongoregi mo lefelong le le esi, kgotsa matsatsi a le 20 – fa e le gore bobedi bo nna kwa diporofenseng tse di farologaneng, go tsibogela taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo,” o ne a tlaleletsa jalo Peta.
Gang fa kgetse e setse e butswe, mongongoregi o tla busediwa ditokomane, mme ene o tla tshwanelwa ke go isa dikarolo di le pedi tsa ditokomane tseno kwa go moromiwa wa kgotlatshekelo mo kgaolong eo  mosekisiwa a nnang kgotsa a dirang kwa go yona.
“Moromiwa wa kgotlatshekelo o tla isa ditokomane tseno ka namana kwa go mosekisiwa  le go mo fapha ka setlankana sa go amogela ditirelo, se se supang gore tirelo ya go tlisa ditokomane kwa go ene e dirilwe. Fa e le gore le na le bana, Katoro ya Babue ledi ba Merero ya Malapa e tla tshwanelwa ke go itsisiwe ka ga seno,” Peta o ne a nopola seno. Morago ga sebaka sa matsatsi a le 10 kgotsa a le 20, mongongoregi a ka tlhatlhela kgetse ya go kgaogana mo kgolaganong ya lenyalo kwa lenaneong la dikgetse tse di letileng tsheko kwa kgotlatshekelo. Mo dikgetseng tsa go tswa mo lenyalong tse e leng gore yo mongwe o santse a kakatletse a ganana le go tswa mo lenyalong,  mose kisiwa o tla tshwanelwa ke go araba taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo ka mokgwa wa go tlhagisa phisegelo yagagwe ya go ganetsana le kgato eno.
“Fa a dirile jalo, o tla tshwanelwa le ke go tlhatlhela boikuelo mo lobakeng lo lo rileng. Boi kuelo ke setlankana se mosekisiwa a  tsibogelang ka sona di tatofatso tse a rwesiwang tsona tse di tlhagelelang ka fa tokoma neng ya taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo.
Gang fa boikuelo bo setse bo tlhatlhetswe, mongongoregi le ene a ka tsibogela boikuelo jono kgotsa a dira kopo ya gore bo kgapelwe kwa thoko.
“Seno se tla tlhotlha gore kgang eno e  kwadisiwe gore e tsene mo lenaneong la ditsheko,” Peta o ne a garela ka go rialo .  vGo tswa mo kgola ganong ya lenyalo moroba go raya eng?Kgotlatshekelo e tla tsibogela kopo ya go tswa mo lenyalong fa e le gore motho yo ba mo rometseng taelo ya go iponagatsa kwa kgotlatshekelo ga a tsibogele taelo eno ka nako e a e neilweng mme ba e mo neetse mo letsogong. Le fa go le jalo, o elediwa gore o ikgolaganye le mokapelo wa gago pele o ka tsaya kgato eno gonne mo tsamaong ya nako a ka tsosa mmudubudu le go tsosolosa kgetse eno ka gore o ne a na le mabaka a a utlwagalang ka jalo ke ka foo a reteletsweng ke go tsibogela taelo eno ka nako.
