  #ThumaMinaSakhela Ilutjha Lekhethu Ingomuso Elinepumelelo INyanga YeLutjha igi dingwa qobe mnya ka ngoMgwengweni njengesikhumbuzo sesi bindi selutjha leSewula Afrika elatjhagala langena eentradeni mhlana ali-16 kuMgwengweni ngomnya ka we-1976 lilwisana noku fundiswa ngelimi lesiBhuru  eenkolweni zekhethu.
Ngikholwa ukuthi loyo mmoya wokulwa usese kho na nanamhlanje esizuku lwaneni esitjha esililutjha lanamhlanje, lona esele lisi lwa nomtlhago osisisahlele ko, ukutlhogeka komsebenzi kunye nokutjhiyana ngendi ma komphakathi.
Abantu abatjhaba, abane tjisakalo engapheliko yoku fikelela epilweni engcono, batjengise ukuncama kwa bo  nokubambelela kwabo lokhuya nababuthanako baba ndawonye bezwakali sa isililo sabo sefundo yasi mahla yemazikweni aphake meko.
Ukwakhiwa kweSewula Afrika enzinzileko nephu melelako ephila ngomnotho oqalelela umuntu woke wona msebenzi ophambi li eendabeni urhulumende ekufuze azitjheje kobanyana azokuphumelela ukwakhela umuntu woke ipilo engcono.
Eenkhathini ezidlulileko si yibuyelele ngokwelakanywe kabili imizamo yethu yoku thuthukisa besihlomise ilu tjha kobanyana lizokwazi ukuhlumisa umnotho, nana mhlanje solo sisaragela phambili ngokwenza njalo khona izakuphumelela imi zamo yethu.
Nanyana ilutjha lilinengi ukudlula woke umuntu esi tjhabeni seSewula Afrika nje, kodwana solo ngilo elingasebenziko ngobune ngi, ngilo elichake khulu nelitjhiywa ngendima uku dlula boke abanye. Nana mhlanje solo ngilo ilutjha elibhokelwe zizifo khulu, ngilo elibongazimbi bobu lelesi benturhu, ngilo elica phazeka ngokungena nge hloko eendakamizweni, ngo kunjalo godu ngilo elisalele emva khulu ngetuthuko.
Njengorhulumende otjhe jako, sisiphalaza ngama ndla sisebenzela ukuqinise kisa ukuthi abantu abatjha bathola amakghonofundwa nelemuko, bathole neseke lo elifaneleko lokungena emabhizinisini kube ngibo abakhanda nabavula imise benzi kunokobanyana babe babantu abafuna umsebenzi, abazakuqatjhwa ngabanye.
Nyakenye ngihlome iJima lezemiSebenzi yokuQatjhwa kweLutjha (i-YES), elinqo phe ukulungiselela ilutjha ukusebenza libe libahlomise nangamakghonofundwa  wechwephetjhe afuneka ko azakusekela ukwanda kwamabubulo emnothweni welizwe.
Ihlelo i-YES limuzamo ohlanganyelwe ngurhu lumende, iinhlangano  zeensebenzi kunye nama bhizinisi kobanyana lizo kunikela isigidi sabantu abatjha abamaSewula Afri ka ilemuko lomsebenzi obhadelako, eminyakeni emithathu ezako. Irhubhu lulo litjengisa ukuthi eba ntwini abaziingidi ezili-15.5 beminyaka yobudala ehla ngana keli-18 nama-34 eSe wula Afrika, abaziingidi ezi5.8 babo abasebenzi.
Ngokungena kweenka mpa-ni ezikulu ngobune ngi ehlelweneli, i-YES  bekube nje seyivulele ilutjha elingasebenziko imisebenzi ezii-17 000.
Ebantwini abatjha abane tjisakalo yamabhizinisi, urhulumende uvule ihle lo lokuthuthukisa ilutjha, i-Ejensi yeliZweloke yoku Thuthukiswa  kweLutjha  ( i-NYDA),  ehlomisa aba ntu abatjha ngamakghonofu ndwa, ngezeemali zetuthu ko, zokuthuthukisa  nokwa kha ilutjha emsebenzini walo, nesekelo kunye noku sebenzisana eemakethe.
Zingaphezulu kwee-2 500 iinkhampani ezisasikima ko esele zisizwe ngeemali, kwavuleka imisebenzi enga phezulu kwee-10 000 emno thweni.
Nasizakuphumelelisa  ngokupheleleko ukusebe nzisana hlangana kwakarhu lumende nekoro yangeqadi, kufuze siqinisekise ukuthi ilutjha elimaSewula Afrika lilungiseleleke ngefanelo ukufaka isandla kiwo woke amakoro womnotho. Kunga kho-ke sizibekela efundweni yelutjha lekhethu.
 Sijame sisimelele ngoku qinisekisa ukuthi boke abentwana, ngaphandle kokukhetha ubujamo babo, abadinywa ithuba lokutho la ifundo yezinga elihle.  Lokhu-ke kungezelelwa na nga mzamo wethu wokwe nza izinga lethu lokufunda nokufundisa libe ngcono ee nkolweni zethu nemazikwe ni wefundo ephakemeko.
Phambilini kiwo umnyaka lo, umNyango wezeFundo ePhakemeko nokuBandula umemezele ukuthi ukhuphe imali eziingidi ezima-R967 ezakubhadela ‘isikolodo sa bafundi sakade’.
  Imali le-ke izakusiza  abafundi abakoloda imali yokufunda yeminyaka edlu lileko  kobanyana isikolodo sabeso singazobavimbela ukufunda emaziko wefundo ephakemeko.
Njengoba urhulumende asebenzisa imali nemitho mbo yamandla engaka nje ukuphumelelisa amandla wetuthuko welutjha, ilu tjha nalo kufuze liveze lalo ihlangothi ekuthuthukiseni iSewula Afrika. Ilutjha ku fuze lihlale licabanga ngemi sebenzi ezakusiza ukwenza iSewula Afrika ibe yindawo engcono nephephileko  yokuphila komuntu woke.  Abanye babantu abatjha bekhethwapha basemahle lweni akarisa okurarako, basemabhizinisini amancani akhuthaza itjisakalo yokura ga ibhizinisi, ngokunjalo godu abanye basemahlelwe ni wokulima nokufuya alu lukezako eendaweni zema khabo.
Bathethe isiqunto  sokukhambela phambili sokwenza umsebenzi obo nakalako ekuthuthukiseni ilizwe nomnotho wekhethu.
Kufuze sikhuthaze nabanye abantu abatjha ukuthi  bafune iindlela zokusebe nzisa amathalentha wabo namakghonofundwa wabo ukuzivulela amabhizinisi babe bavule nemisebenzi.
Ukwenza nokuthwa la umsebenzi omuhle  nohlonipheke kangaka  kukhambisana nokubale kela ukungena ngehloko e tjwaleni neendakamizweni kunye nokukhambela kude nobulelesi nobukhohlakali.
Ngaphezu kwakho koke, abantu abatjha kufuze ba zibabalele bangangenwa yiNgogwana yeNtumba ntonga (i-HIV) nezinye izifo zomseme; lokho-ke kufuze bakwenze ngokusebenzisa amakhondomu, nangokuzila ukuya emsemeni ngaphambi kwesikhathi basese bancani, kunye nangokunande bazi hlolisa ipilwabo yomzimba.
Ingomuso leSewula Afrika lisezandleni zelutjha elizibophelele ngokwakha umphakathi ohlukileko nongcono kunaloyo ilutjha langomnyaka we-1976 elive la kiwo. Asisebenzisaneni ukwakha nokuphumelelisa ingomuso elingcono lelutjha lekhethu. 3UMgwengweni 2019 UmGadangiso 2
