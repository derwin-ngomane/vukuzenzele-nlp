  #ThumaMinaKwakhela lusha lwetfu likusasa lelinconoINyanga Yelusha igu jwa njalo ngemnyaka ngenyanga yeNhlaba kukhombisa kuhlonipha umoya wekungasabi  kwelusha laseNingizimu Afrika lelagcwala imigwa co mhla ti-16 Inhlaba 1976 lalwela kucedza kufundzi swa ngeSibhunu etikolwe ni tetfu.  Ngiyatsemba kutsi lowo moya wekulwa usaphila kusitukulwane lesisha se bantfu labasha, labo nyalo labalwela inkinga yebu phuya, kuswelakala kwe msebenti kanye neku ngalingani. Labantfu labasha labane sifiso lesingeke siphumele le kalula semphilo lencono, bakhombise kutibophelela nekubeketela uma bahla ngana belwela imfundvo lephakeme yamahhala.
Kuphumelela kwakha iNingizimu Afrika lene mphumelelo lesimeme na lenemnotfo lofaka wonkhe umuntfu kuyintfo lecali swa embili kuloluhlelo lwa hulumende lekucinisekisa  imphilo lencono kubo bo nkhe bantfu. Siphu melele futsi sitawu chubeka kwengeta  imitamo yetfu yekutfutfu kisa nekunika lusha  emandla kute lukhone ku faka sandla ekukhuliseni live letfu.Nanoma ngabe luyince nye lenkhulu yebantfu ba seNingizimu Afrika, lusha luyachubeka kuhlangabe tana nenkinga yekungase benti, yebuphuya kanye neyekungalingani. Kuhla la kungilo lelihlangabetana kakhulu netifo, bugebengu lebunebudlova, kusebenti sa tidzakwamiva kanye nekungatfutfuki ngaloku fanele.
Njengahulumende lo nakekelako, sisebenta nge kutikhandla kucinisekisa kutsi bantfu labasha batfo la emakhono nesipiliyoni, futsi batfola kwesekelwa lokudzingekako kute ba ngene kumkhakha wema bhizinisi babe bacashi kunekutsi babe tisebenti.
Lomnyaka lophelile, ngi sungule Luphiko Lweku cashwa Kwelusha (i-YES), lelihlose kulungiselela bantfu labasha kutsi ba tfole umsebenti nekubani ka emakhono ebungcweti ladzingekako kute babe yincenye yekutfutfukisa umnotfo.
Loluhlelo lwe-YES lingu mtamo lohlanganisa hu lumende, mkhakha we temisebenti nemabhizini si lonika bantfu labasha labasigidzi baseNingi zimu Afrika misebenti le khokhelako kuleminyaka lemitsatfu letako. Ngekuya kwelucwaningo, ebantfwi ni baseNingizimu Afri ka labatigidzi le-15.5 la baneminyaka yebudzala lesemkhatsini yale-18 na lenge-34, labatigidzi leti5.8 abasebenti.
Njengoba kunetinkhapa ni letinkhulu letingenile, lemtamo we-YES kute kube kunyalo uvulele bantfu la basha labangasebenti ema tfuba emsebenti la-17 000.
Bantfu labasha labanesifi so sekuba bosomabhizini si, hulumende wasungula i-Ejensi Yavelonkhe Yeku tfutfukisa Lusha (i-NY DA), lesebenta kuceceshela emakhono, kusita ngemali yekutfutfukisa, lekuniketa umuntfu lotakufundzisa msebenti, kusekela futsi nekuchumanisa nebantfu labatawutsenga imikhicito yabo.
Kunetinkhapani letinge tulu kwe-2 500 letinsha le tiniketwe imali yekucala ibhizinisi, letivule imise benti lengetulu kwe-10 000 kumnotfo.
Uma sifuna kubusebe ntisa kahle lobudlelwana bahulumende nemkhakha wetemabhizinisi angasese, kufuneka sicinisekise ku tsi lusha lwaseNingizimu Afrika linikwa emakhono lafanele ekungena kuyo yonkhe imikhakha yemno tfo. Kungako sisisa kumfu ndvo yelusha lalive.
Sitimisele kucinisekisa kutsi bonkhe bantfwana, nanoma ngabe simo sinjani, abancishwa litfuba lekutfo la imfundvwo lesezingeni lelisetulu. Loku kungetwa ngumtamo wetfu wekutfu tfukisa lizinga lekufundza nekufundzisa etikolweni tetfu nakutikhungo temfu ndvo lephakeme.
Ekucaleni kwalomnya ka, Litiko Letemfundo Le phakeme Nekucecesha lam emetela kunikwa kwetigid zi leti-R967 kute kubhadal we tindleko tekufundza kubafundzi ‘labanetikwe leti teminyaka leyengcile.’Lemali itawusita bafu ndzi labebakweleta tindle ko tekufundza kusuka  kumnyaka lowengca  ngekucinisekisa kutsi si kweleti asibavimbeli kutsi batfole imfundvwo lepha keme.  Kuko konkhe lokusi sa lokwentiwe nguhulu mende kute kutfutfukiswe lusha, lusha nalo kufanele ludlale indzima yalo eku tfutfukiseni iNingizimu  Afrika. Kufuneka lube neti ntfo lelutentako letengeta kwenta lelive libe ngule lincono naleliphephile kute wonkhe umuntfu aphile kulo. Labanye bantfu betfu labasha bamatasatasa nge miklamo lemangalisako, ngemabhizinisi lamanca ne kanye nangekulima emi mangweni yangakubo.
Batsatse sincumo lesise bentako lesidlala indzima lemcoka kuchubekisela live phambili kanye nekukhu lisa umnotfo. Kufuneka sigcugcutele bantfu laba sha labaningi kutsi babuke tindlela tekusebentisa li thalentha nemakhono abo kute basungule emabhizi nisi abo lamancane bakhe nemisebenti. Umsebenti lohloniphekile njengalona ufuna ungana tsi tjwala futsi ungasebentisi tidzakwamiva, futsi unga bi yincenye yebugebengu nenkhohlakalo.
Lokunye lokubaluleke kakhulu, bantfu labasha kufuneka batigcine baphe phile kuSandvulelangcu lazi (i-HIV) naletinye tifo temacansi letitsatselanako ngekutsi basebentise lija zi lemkhwenyana (ikho ndomu), bagweme licansi basesebancane nangeku tsi bahlale baya kuyohlola njalo.
Likusasa leNingizimu Af rika lisetandleni telusha lolutibophelele ekwakheni sive lesihlukile kuleso  lusha lwemnyaka we1976 libuya kuso. Asise bentisa neni kute lusha lwetfu lube nelikusasa  lelincono.   3Inhlaba 2019 Lushicilelo 2
