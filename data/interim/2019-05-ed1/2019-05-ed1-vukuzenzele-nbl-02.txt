Vuk’uzenzeleIINZUZO ZEKULULEKOUrhulumende uzi bophelele ekukhu tjhulweni kwehlelo lokuThuthukiswa kwabe Ntwana KwebuNcanini  (i-ECD), elithathwa ephasini mazombe njengethulusi lo kusisa ekufukulweni kwaba ntu elingasetjenziswa lilizwe.
“Nasizimisele ngokuphelisa umtlhago kufuze sifundise abantwana bemindeni etlha gako,” kwatjho uMengameli u-Cyril Ramaphosa eKulu meni yakhe yobuJamo be Narha (i-SoNA) yomnyaka wee-2018.
Ukutlhayelelwa mathuba nokungafaki isandla ema bangeni aphasi wabentwa na kungaqintelisa abentwa na khulu kutjhabalalise namathuba wabo wepume lelo.
Nanyana kunjalo, ngomnya ka wee-2009 usolwazi weECD weYunivesithi yeTlha gwini Tjingalanga uDorh.Ju liana Seleti, lokha nakakhu luma kusiFundobandulo seBhanka yePhasiloke ebesi mayelana nokuThuthukiswa nokuTjhejwa kwabeNtwana abaNcani e-Afrika wathi ihle lo lebandlululo langapha mbilini komnyaka we-1994 khange litjhiye abentwa na ngaphandle.
Gade kunganamithetho mgomo ye-ECD, ngitjho nebentwaneni babantu aba ngakhange badinywe ama thuba, gade kuneensiza zeECD ezisabaleleko lapho kwazuza abantwana abamba lwa.
Wathi ukutlolwa kwemi thethomgomo ehlelekileko kwathoma eminyakeni ye1990 ngemva kokutjhatjhu lulwa ejele kukaMengameli wangaphambilini u-Nelson Mandela.
Ngomnyaka wee-2003 u mNyango wezokuThuthu kiswa komPhakathi, weze -Fundo esiSekelo newezama Philo yazitlamela imithetho mgomo ye-ECD; kwatla nywa amayunithi namaphiko ahleleke kuhle nokuseke lwa ngemali kwe-ECD kwa ba yingcenye yeSabelomali ukuthoma ngomnyaka wee2000.
I-ECD seyimaqaloqangi  karhulumende kanti iKha binethi nayo ivumele umThe thogomo oHlangeneko weECD weliZweloke ngoNo bayeni wee-2015, ukutjhugu lula ukulethwa kwezenzelwa ze-ECD eSewula Afrika ngo kutjheja iinkhala eziqaka thekileko nokuqinisekisa ngokufumaneka kweze nzelwa ze-ECD ezipheleleko, ezifaneleko nezifunyanwa yoke indawo.
I-ECD ifuduselwa eMnyangweni wezeFundo esiSekeloUmsebenzi wamaziko we-ECD ususiwe eMnyangweni wezo kuThuthukiswa komPhakathi wafuduselwa kewezeFundo esiSekelo, lapho kunabentwa na abangaphezu kwee-700 000 abafumana izenzelwa ze-ECD ngelizweni mazombe.
“Leli ligadango eliqakathe kileko kuragelophambili leze fundo yabentwana elizweni lekhethu, ekungathi nalethu lwe ngefanelo, kuzakuba ligadango eliqakathekileko ukufikelela inembombono yomnyaka wee-2030 yomTla mo wokuThuthukiswa kweli Zweloke (i-NDP). Nokho nga phandle kwehlelo lokusebenza elinqophileko namagadango aphathekako, isimemezelo lesi angeze saletha amarhubhelo elizweni nofana kilutjha”, kwatjho uMengameli Rama phosa.
Igadango elilandelako mi nyaka emibili we-ECD ekate lelweko kibo boke abentwana ukulungiselela iGreyidi yoku-1, ukuya ngokwesimemezelo sikaMengameli uRamaphosa ku-SoNA yakhe yomnyaka wee-2019.
“Elinye iqaloqangi eliqaka thekileko kuthuthukisa ngokubonakalako ikghono lokufunda ngokuzwisisa emi nyakeni yokuthoma yesikolo.
Lokhu kuqakathekile ekuhlo miseni abentwana ukuphume lela kezefundo, emsebenzini nepilweni okuyinto eqakatheke khulu ekulwisaneni nomtlha go, ukutlhayela kwemisebenzi nokungalingani”, kwatjho uMengameli.
Wangezelela ngokuthi, “Sizokwandisa ngokubona kalako iinsetjenziswa zokufu nda zamabanga aphasi esi gabeni esiyisisekelo godu eminyakeni esithandathu ezako sizokunikela woke umntwana wesikolo eSewula Afrika iincwadi zomsebe nzi zedijithali nezokufunda kumatablet . Sizokuthoma ngeenkolo lezo ezadinywa amathuba ngaphambilini nezisemiphakathini etlhaga ko, kufaka hlangana iinkolo zamagreyidi ahlangeneko nezemikhakha ehlangeneko, zemaplasini nezemakhaya.
UNgqongqotjhe wezeFundo esiSekelo u-Angie Motshekga uthe umnyango sele ulungele ukwethula ngeengaba iincwa di zedijithali ukuthoma ngo mnyaka ozako.
NgoNtaka wee-2019 uMnya ngo wezeFundo esiSekelo – ngokutjhebisana ne-Vodacom Foundation, i-Nelson Mandela Foundation, uMnyango we zaboMma weHlangano yee nTjhaba eziBumbeneko (i-UN) ne-Global Citizen –banikela ngeSentha ye-ECD eDivhani Creche ne-Frank Ravele Seco ndary School of Excellence e-Vhembe, ese-Limpopo.
Ikulisa le, ingenye yezili-15 za masentha we-ECD ekhethelwe ukuvuselelwa ngomthangala sisekelo omutjha.
