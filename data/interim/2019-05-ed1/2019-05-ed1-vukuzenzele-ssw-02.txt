Vuk’uzenzeleTITSELO TENKHULULEKOHulumende utinikele eLuhlelweni Lwe mfundvo Yasenkhu lisa (i-ECD) lolutsatseka mhlaba wonkhe njengelu hlelo lolubaluleke kakhulu  eluntfwini live lelungalenta.
“Nangabe sifuna kucedza buphuya, kudzingeka ku tsi sifundzise bantfwana balabo labaphuyile,” ku sho Mengameli Cyril Rama phosa eNkhulumeni yakhe Yebunjalo Belive (i-SoNA) yanga-2018.
Kuswelakala kwematfuba nelusito eminyakeni yeku cala yekukhula kwemntfwa na kungabalahlekisela ka khulu bantfwana labancane futsi kunciphisa ematfuba abo ekuphumelela.
Nanome kunjalo, nga-2009 ingcweti ye-ECD yaseNyu vesi yaseNyakatfo Nsho nalanga, Dkt. Juliana Seleti, watsi ngesikhatsi kubanjwe uMhlanganosikolo weLibha nge Lemhlaba lomayelana Nemtamo Wekunakeke lwa Nekutfutfukiswa Kwe bantfwana e-Afrika luhlelo lwelubandlululo lwange mbikwa-1994 alumange  lubashiye ngephandle ba ntfwana kuba nemtselela lo mubi kubo.
Bekute tinchubomgomo teECD, ngisho netebantfwana balabo labanotsile, bekukho na nje tinsita te-ECD teba ntfu labambalwa, bantfwana bebambalwa kakhulu labe bazuza ngato.
Tinchubomgomo te-ECD letifanele lapha eNingizi mu Afrika tacala ekucaleni kweminyaka yabo-1990  ngesikhatsi Mengameli  waphambilini Nelson Mande la akhululwa ejele.
Watsi nga-2003 Litiko Lete kutfutfukiswa Kwetenhla lakahle, Letemfundvo Lesise kelo neLetemphilo asungule tawo tinchubomgomo teECD; kwavulwa mayunithi nemaphiko lafanele kwatsi kusekelwa ngetimali kweECD kwacala kwaba yintfo levelako manje kusabeloti mali semnyaka wanga-2000.
I-ECD seyiyintfo hulu mende layibeke embili kutsi utawucalisa ngayo kuye nta futsi neKhabhinethi iyivumile Inchubomgomo Yavelonkhe Lehlanganisi we ye-ECD ngeNgongoni 2015, letawugucula kule tfwa kwetinsita te-ECD lapha eNingizimu Afrika ngekutsi kulungiswe emagebe lamco ka kanye nekucinisekisa kuletfwa kwetinsita te-ECD letiphelele, letitfolakala nome ngabe kukuphi futsi letilinganako.I-ECD iba ngephasi kweMnyango Wemfundvo LesisekeloLoko lokwentiwa tikhungo te-ECD sekusukile ngaphasi kweMnyango Wetekutfutfu kiswa Kwetenhlalakahle kwaya kuMnyango Wemfu ndvo Lesisekelo, nebantfwa na labangetulu kwe-700 000 kwanyalo labatfola tinsita teECD eveni lonkhe.
“Lesisinyatselo lesimcoka se nchubekelembili emfundvwe ni yebantfwana labancane kule live letfu, nangabe sifezekiswe ngalokuphelele sitawuba sinya tselo lesimcoka sekufezekisa umbononchanti weLuhlelo Lwekutfutfukisa Lwavelonkhe (i-NDP) lwanga-2030. Nanome kunjalo, ngaphandle kwemasu ekucala kusebenta lacacako kanye netindlela tekukwenta letiphatsekako, lesimemetelo ngeke sibe nenzuzo kulelive nome ngeke bazuze lutfo bantfwana labancane balelive,” kwasho Mengameli Ramapho sa.
Sinyatselo lesilandzelako kulenchubo, nguleminyaka lemibili ye-ECD lephocelekile kutsi kubo bonkhe bantfwana, ekubalungiseleleni Libanga Lekucala, njengaloku kumeme tele Mengameli Ramaphosa eNkhulumeni yakhe Yebunjalo Belive yanga-2019.
“Lokunye lokumcoka  lokubekwe embili kutsi kucalwe ngako kwentiwe kutfutfukisa kakhulu ku fundzela kuvisisa eminyakeni yekucala yekungena sikolo.
Loku kumcoka ekuhlomiseni bantfwana kutsi baphumelele emfundvweni, emisebentini kanye nasemphilweni futsi ngilo liphuzu linye lelimcoka lekuncoba buphuya, kuswela imisebenti nekungalingani,” kwasho yena njalo Mengameli.
“Sitawukwandzisa kakhulu tinsitakufundza temabanga ekucala esigabeni sesisekelo.
Kuleminyaka lesitfupha letako sitawunika wonkhe umntfwa na losesikolweni lapha eNi ngizimu Afrika tincwadzi tekusebentela netekufundza te-elekthronikhi kumatablet .
Sitawucala ngaleto tikolo le tancisheka ematfuba phambi lini futsi letisemimangweni le phuye kakhulu, lokufaka ekhatsi tikolo letifundzisa ema banga netigaba letinengi, tase mapulazini netasetindzweni tasemaphandleni,” kwengeta yena njalo.
Indvuna Yemfundvo Lesise kelo Angie Motshekga utsite litiko lakhe likulungele kufaka tincwadzi tekufundza leti-elek thronikhi kusukela kulomnya ka lotako.
NgeNdlovulenkhulu 2019 Litiko Lemfundvo Lesisekelo – ngekuhlanganyela neVo dacom Foundation, i-Nelson Mandela Foundation, Luphiko Labomake laMhlabuhlangene (i-UN) ne-Global Citizen – bandlulisele Sikhungo se-ECD eDivhani Crèche kanye nase Ravele Secondary School of Exce llence eVhembe, eLimpopo.
Lenkhulisa, ingulenye yetikhungo te-ECD leti-15 lekhetfwe kutsi ivusetelwe kabusha, itfole sakhiwonchanti sekuyenta kabusha lesiphelele.
I-Frank Ravele Secondary School ngulesinye setikolo kuleti-12 seTikolo Telizinga Lelisetulu letikhetfwe Litiko Lemfundvo Lesisekelo ngeku hlanganyela ne-Vodacom, le seyivele ichumanise tikolo leti3000 netikhungo tabothishela letinge-92 eveni lonkhe. v“Nangabe sifuna kucedza buphuya, kudzingeka kutsi sifundzise bantfwana balabo labaphuyile,” Tinsuku leti-1000 tekucala temntfwana tigcile ekudleni lokwanele, kunakekela, kuphepha kanye nekuvusa imiva. Kubantfwana labaneminyaka yebudzala lesukela kulemibili kuya kulesihlanu, kugcilwa ekuvuseni imiva lokusezingeni kanye nekunakekelwa, kulapho-ke i-ECD ifike idlale khona indzima lemcoka.Hulumende wesekela Luhlelo Lwemfundvo Yasenkhulisa Kutfutfukisa bantfwana• Litiko Letetimali liyachubeka nekusekela ngetimali i-ECD ngekusebentisa sibonelelo lesibekelwe eceleni kwenta loko.
