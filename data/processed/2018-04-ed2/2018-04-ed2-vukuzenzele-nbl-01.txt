Ukukhandelwa Kwesomiso Esikhulu

NJENGOMBANA SEKUMENYEZELWE  bona isomiswesi siyihlekelele yelizweloke, i-Vuk’uzenzele ingenelela emirajini kanobangela wesomiswesi nokobana ngiyiphi imizamo yokongiwa  kwamanzi eyenziwako.

Njengombana isomiso sesifikelele ezingeni lehlekelele enje eTlha gwini Kapa nePumalanga Kapa lapho amazinga wama damu kamasipala we-Nelson Mandela Bay asema-25%, kusi khathi sokobana amaSewula Afrika onge lesisisetjenziswa esiyindlala nesiligugu.
Isomiso sikhathi eside khulu sokungani kwezulu okubangela ukutlhayelelwa mamanzi.
Ngokutjho kukaDorh. Mathieu Roualt, omsizi kaphrofesa emNyangweni weemFundo eziPhathelene nezamaLwandle (i-Oceonography) eYunivesithi yeKapa, unobangela omkhulu wesomiso mithelela yeEl Niño nokutjhugutjhuguluka kobuja mo bezulu nanyana itlayimethi.
Wathi “I-El Niño  sisehlakalo semvelo esibuyelela njalo ngem va kweminyaka emithathu uku ya kemihlanu godu ubujamobu bungathatha iminyaka emibili.
Bandisa umtjhiso emalwandle we-Pacific ne-Indian, okunga bangela ubujamo besomiso”.
Ukutjhugutjhuguluka kwe tlayimethi kuhlathululwa nje ngamatjhuguluko wama  phetheni wetlayimethi  yephasi zombelele okwa thoma phakathi ukuya  ngekupheleni komnya kakhulu nanyana isentjhuri yama-20 godu kungunobangela wokukhula kwamazinga wo mmoya osilaphazekileko emka yini (ikhaboni dayioksa yidi) okhiqizwa kusetjenziswa kwerhasi yemvelo.
UDorh. Roualt wahlathulula ngokuthi “Isentjhuri edluli leko le i-Afrika engeSewula itlhage khulu ngamatjhugu tjhuguluko wetlayimethi waqobe mnyakaokwabangela isomiso esirhageleko nokupha zamiseka kwepilo yeemila neentjalo zequlwanini nezema nzini nepilo yabantu ngo kwabo. Amatjhuguluko la abanomthelela ebubulweni lezokulima, amadamu nem nothweni wezokuthiya nemno thweni ngokuphele leko. Godu amatjhuguluko la athikameza nokugeleza kwemi lanjana nezakhipilo zeentjalo ezitjhinga elwandle,”.
Imithelela leiliduma elin gophiko phezu kweSewula Afrika khulukhulu ngombana ilizwe lekhethu linendlala ye zulu elingaba mamililitha ama450 (ml) nalimadaniswa nezulu elina ephasini elimamililitha ama-860.
Iyini Imithelela Yesomiso?Ngenyanga kaMhlolanja umN yango wezokuBusa ngokuBa mbisana nezeNdabuko (i-Cogta) utjhugulule izinga lobujamo besomisa ukusuka ekubeni yihlekelele yesifunda ukuya ekubeni yihlekelele yeli zweloke.
UDorh. Mmaphaka Tau, oyi hloko yeZiko lokuLawulwa kweHlekelele leliZweloke (NDMC) uthe “ngemva kokuhlahluba kabutjha ubu jamo oburhagalako besomiso eemfundeni eentathu, iim phathimandla zezehlekelele zibuyekeze ubujamo besomiso bamenyezelwa njengehlekelele yelizweloke”.  Nanyana kubudisi nje ukuli nganisa imithelela yesomiso kezomnotho, kodwana kube nomthelela omumbi omkhulu emkhakheni wezokulima. Nge Tjingalanga Kapa, esebujameni besibili ngelizweni ngengeniso yomKhiqizo wangeKhaya oPhe leleko (i-GDP) kezokulima, ku lahleke imisebenzi epheze ibe zii-21 000 ekoteni yesithathu nyakenye. Umkhiqizo wekoroyi  wehle ngama-47% ngomnyaka wee-2017 begodu kunekgho nakalo yokutlhayela kwewayini ngamalitha aziingidi ezima-90  ezakuthunyelwa emazweni wangaphandle nethengiswa ngelizweni lekhethu.  Ukutlhayela kwamanzi ku zakuba nomthelela omumbi  omkhulu nekukhanjisweni kwendle nanyana ekuhlwe ngiseni, emkhakheni wetjhejo lezamaphilo newezokuvaka tjha.
Khuyini Okwenziwako Ukuqalana Nobujamobu?Abomasipala abasahlelwe siso miso bamemezele imithetho eqinileko yokonga amanzi ngokomkhawulo othileko efanele ilandelwe zizakhamuzi nebamabhizinisi.
Amaziko wokuhlanza amanzi welwandle ukobana alungele ukusetjenziswa mphakathi, amahlelo wokulungisa  amaphayiphi, ukusetjenziswa kwamanzi kwehlandla lesibili nokwenjiwa kweempetsi za manzi kumagadango aqalele lweko bona azakuba yipengu njengombana amaphrojekthi la sele aya ngekupheleni.

I-App Yenza ngcono zokuthutha e-Gauteng

I-APP ETJHA yenza kube lula ukusebenzisa iinthuthi zomphakathi.

UmNyango we zeeNdlela neen Thuthi we-Gau teng uthuthukise ihlelo lawo leenthuthi zompha kathi ngokuhloma i-App  ebizwa nge-Gauteng on the Move ezokusiza aba  khweli ukusebenzisa  lula imihlobohlobo yeenthuthi.
I-Gauteng on the Move  izokuphelisa ukuzaza okubangelwa ziinthuthi zomphakathi ngombana inikela abakhweli ilwazi elinqophileko ngeenkhathi zokukhamba kweenthuthi nemali ebhadelwako  ye-Gautrain‚ i-Metrobus‚ i-A Re Yeng‚ i-Rea Vaya‚ i-Me trorail‚ iimBhesi ze-Gautrain‚ i-Johannesburg City Sight seeing Bus (ekuyibhesi yokubukela iindawo), iimB hesi ze-Tshwane nebubulo lamateksi.
Nagade akhuluma  mhlapha emnyanyeni wokuhloywa kwe-App le ema-ofisini wabatlami bayo e-Midrand, uSomkhandlu wezokuThutha e-Gauteng, u-Ismail Vadi uthe umnya ngo ubone isidingo sokuse tjenziswa kwethekhnoloji ehle nehlelo leenthuthi elithuthukileko ngomnqo pho wokuthuthukisa ihlelo leenthuthi.
“URhulumende wesi Funda se-Gauteng unikele ngesikhathi, amano nee nsetjenziswa ekuthuthuki sweni kwalelihlelo elisiza abakhweli ukuhlela lula amakhambabo ngemihlobo yeenthuthi ehlukahlukeneko kilesisifunda. Ubeke wathi, "Sibona amandla wamano wethekhnoloji akha kabu tjha iinthuthi zomphakathi.
Asingabazi bonyana ama tjhuguluko wethekhnoloji ephasini atjhugulula umkha kha wezeenthuthi. Kufuze sizijayeze amatjhuguluko la besikhambisane nawo”.
U-Vadi uqinisekise bo nyana isifunda sizakura ga nokusebenzisa amano wezerhubhululo netuthuko ngomnqopho wokwenza ngcono ukusebenza kuhle nokufikeleleka kweenthuthi.
Nasele idawunilowudiwe i-App le izakusiza abase benzisi bayo ukuhlela amakhambabo ngokukhe tha umhlobo wesithuthi abasitlhogako, iinkhathi zokukhamba nokhunye aba kutlhogako njengekhambo elifitjhani nanyana elitjhi phileko.
Umfundi nomkhweli weenthuthi zomphakathi eJwanasbhege u-Glen Dlamini uthe i-App le izo kwethula iinkulungwana ngeenkulungwana zaba khweli umthwalo.
“I-App le imkarisomraro.
Iliqhinga likarhulumende eliletha amatjhuguluko, khulukhulu kulutjha elifa na nami ngisuka e-Soweto ngtjhinge eYunivesithi yeJwanasbhege enge-Auck land Park qobe lilanga.
“Gadesi ngikghona uku hlela amakhambo wami ngcono ngokwemali engi nayo bengenze isilinganiso seenkhathi zokukhamba.
“I-App le imkarisomraro, ilisizo khulu emuntwini onjengami ongasiso isakha muzi sesifundenesi, njengo mbana kuzokuba lula kimi ukuya eendaweni ezi hlukahlukeneko,” kwatjho u-Khanyo Malunga, nagade akhuluma ne-Vuk’uzenzele  e-Blairgowrie.
Wangezelela ngokuthi, “Nangize lapha nje ngizoku hlungelwa umsebenzi, ngizokukghona ukuhlela ngcono ikhambo lami bengi zijayeze indawo”.
I-App le iyafumaneka ki bofunjathwako be-Android  nebe-iPhone, abafumaneka eentolo zama-App ezihlu kahlukeneko.
