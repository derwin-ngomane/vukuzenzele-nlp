Vuk’uzenzele generalWonke amaphuzu mayelana nokuba nebipolarAllison CooperYize kunezinhlobo ezahlukahlukene ze-bipolar okuyiyo eyinhloko, eyayaziwa ngaphambilini ngokuthi i-manic depression, eyisimo sempilo yengqondo esi bangela ushintsho oludlu lele emizwelo yomuntu kusuka ekuphakameni  ngokomzwelo (i-mania) ukuya ekukhathazekeni komphefumulo (i-depres-sion), kube nezikhathi zemi zwelo ejwayelekile pha kathi kwalokhu.
UDkt Eddie Pak, isazi sezifo zengqondo sesibhedlela soMnyango wezeMpilo waseGauteng, i-Sterkfon-tein Psychiatric Hospital esiseKrugersdorp, uchaze ukuthi kuyinto ejwayelekile neyamukelekile ukuba aba-ntu babe nemizwelo ehlukene futhi bazizwe bejabule noma bedangele, njengoba izinto ezihlukahlukene zenzeka empilweni yabo.
"Isifo se-bipolar, noma ku njalo, kulapho umuntu eba nokuguquguquka kwemi-zwelo yakhe ngokwedlulele, ngaphandle kwesizathu esicacile. Izinguquko zivame ukuba zifike ngezikhawu ezithile, eziyisikhathi eside futhi ziphazamise impilo yansuku zonke," kuqhuba yena.
Izimpawu zebipolarNgokusho komhlahlandlela we-bipolar osungulwe nga be-South African Depres-sion and Anxiety Group (i-SADAG), izimpawu zalezi zikhawu zokuphazamiseka kwengqondo zibandakanya; 
•	Umuzwa wokwenama kakhulu futhi kungekho lutho olungashintsha lo-kho; 
•	Imicabango emihle nge ndlela eseyize yedlulela; 
•	Ukwahluleka ukufunda isimo kahle lokho ke kubeka umuntu onale sifo engozini yokusebenzisa izidakamizwa nokwenza izinto ngokunganaki.
•	Bangenama kakhulu kanga ngokuthi kungadlula izi nsuku eziningi belala nje kancane noma bangalali nhlobo; 
•	Bangaxoxa ngalesi si-hloko badlulele kwesinye engxoxweni eyodwa futhi bakhuluma ngokushesha futhi bekhulumela phezu-lu; 
•	Abanye abakwazi uku-baqonda njengoba imi-cabango yabo nenkulumo yabo ingahlelekile futhi ingawakhi umqondo; 
•	Bangaba nentukuthelo nga-phandle kwesizathu noma lapho othile eveza ukuthi izinhlelo zabo azinamqo ndo.  "Uma leso sikhawu  sokuphakama komzwe-lo ngokudlulele (imania) singelashwa ngesikhathi, singabangela ukuphithana  kwengqondo yomuntu (i-psychosis) nokungaholela eku-theni alahlekelwe yingqondo ngokuphelele. Angase agcine engasakwazi ukuhlukanisa izinto zangokoqobo nezi ngezona zangempela bese eba nentukuthelo nodlame. Iziqephu eziningi zalokhu kugula zilula kakhulu ukuzibona kwabanye, ngoba ziza ngamandla kakhulu," kusho uDkt. Pak.
Lezi ziqephu zilandelwa ukukhathazeka komphefu mulo, okunezimpawu ezifa-nayo nokukhazathazeka oku-vamile noma ukukhathazeka okukhulu okubandakanya ukuzizwa ngalezi ndlela zi-landelayo; 
•	Ukudangala•	Ukuzizwa ulahlwa unembe za noma ungenathemba•	Ukuzizwa ungafuni uku-vuka embhedeni noma ukufuna ukulala kepha esikhathini esiningi unga kwazi ukukwenza lokho•	Ukungabi namandla  nokuzizwa ubuthakathaka futhi ukhathele•	 Ukungakuthandi ukudla; ukulahlekelwa intshisekelo yezinto nokuzizwa unge-lutho.
"Ukuhlaselwa yilesi siqe-phu sokuzizwa ukhatha zekile singase siholele na kwi-psychosis, lapho abantu behluleka ukubona izinto zangokoqobo nezingezona. Uma lesi sifo sibahlasele kakhulu abantu abanaso, baye bafune ukuzilimaza futhi bangaba nomuzwa wokufuna ukuzibulala," kusho uDkt. Pak.
v EZEMPILOI-bipolar : Ungasiza kanjani?Uma ucabanga ukuthi wena noma umuntu osisihlobo sakho unesifo se-bipolar , vakashela umtholampilo wabagula ngengqondo oseduze nawe, isibhedlela noma udokotela ngokushesha.
Izimpawu eziyizinkomba zokufuna ukuzibulala Kubaluleke kakhulu ukufunda izimpawu eziyizinkomba zokufuna ukuzibulala nokuthatha noma yiziphi izinsongo umuntu azenza njengezibucayi.
Uma lo muntu "egoqa" izindaba zakhe, ekhuluma ngokuzibulala, elokhu njalo ekhuluma ngezindlela zokulandelela noma ebonisa imizwa yokuphelelwa yithemba sekuyisikhathi sokungenelela futhi ufune usizo kudokotela noma amalungu omndeni nabangane.  Thola usizo Uma wena noma umuntu omaziyo edinga usizo, vakashela isikhungo sempilo sabagula ngengqondo esiseduze nawe, umtholampilo, isibhedlela noma udokotela.

Ndasa 2019 Ushicilelo 2Allison CooperUma othile osisihlobo sakho ethole ukuthi unesifo se-bipolar kukhona izinto ezihlukahlu kene ongazenza ukuze usize futhi umeseke.
Okokuqala, funda konke okukhona ukuthi ukwazi ngohlobo le-bipolar umuntu usisihlobo sakho atholakale ukuthi enayo. Thola izimba ngela zayo, izimpawu kanye nemithi yokuyelapha bese ukhuluma nodokotela wa-khe uma kungenzeka. Kufanele futhi ufunde mayelana nezimpawu eziyisexwayiso zeziqephu ze-bipo lar zokuphaphazela kakhulu kanye nokuba phansi kakhulu, ukuze wazi ukuthi umuntu  angaqala aziphathe kanjani.
Ngokomhlahlandlela we-bi polar osungulwe ngabe-South African Depression and Anxiety Group (i-SADAG) kuwumqondo omuhle ukuhlela, ngenkathi umuntu engakaguli, ukuthi kufanele wenze njani uma ubona ukuthi usebanjwe yisifo se-bipolar. Isibonelo, ningaxoxa futhi nibeke imi thetho engabandakanya ukumvikela efana nokungamniki amakhadi okuthenga, amalungelo okubhanga no khiye bemoto.
"Khuthaza umuntu onalesi sifo ukuthi angayeki imithi, abonane nodokotela wakhe' futhi agweme utshwala nezidakamizwa njengoba lokhu ku ngavusa noma kubhebheth ekise isimo se-bipolar sibe sibi kakhulu," kusho uDkt. Eddie Pak, isazi sezifo zengqondo sesibhedlela soMnyango WezeM pilo wase-Gauteng i-Sterkfon tein Psychiatric Hospital.
Uma umuntu onale sifo esisebenzise imithi yokwelashwa  ethile isikhathi esi de, kungabonakali bungcono ezimpa wini, noma kune miphumela emi bi, mkhuthaze umuntu lowo ukuba abuze udokotela  mayelana nezi nye izinhlobo zemithi yokwe lasha noma  athole umbono womunye  udokotela.
Baphathe ngendlela ejwayelekile aba-ntu uma sebeluleme, kodwa uhlale uqaphele izimpawu.  v
