Vuk’uzenzeleUkutholakala Kwekankere Yesibeletho Kusese Nesikhathi KuyasindisaNge-NHI: Sisalelwe Ligadango Linye Bona Sembeswa Yingubo Yinye YezamaphiloUKUSUKELA EMINYAKENI YOBUDALA EMA-20, abantu abasikazi kufuze banande bayo kwenza i-pap smear, kobanyana ikankere yesibeletho izokutholakala kungakabi lada ukuyelaphisa. Loke mlayezo orhatjhelwa abantu bengubo njengobanyana uKhukhulamungu kuyiNyanga YokuPhandlulula NgeKankere nje.
UmThethomli ngwa weTjhorensi yokuQalelela zama Philo yeliZweloke (i-NHI) obewulindelwe ngamehlo abovu sewumenyezelwe yiPalamende – ngaleyindlela-ke avulekile amasango wokwamukela imibono nee mphakamiso zomphakathi, newokubonisana nawo ngawo lomthethomlingwa.
Ukumenyezelwa komThetho mlingwa lo kubeka iSewula Afrika egadangweni lokugcina lokuphumelelisa i-NHI.
UmThethomlingwa lo utliki tlwe nguNgqongqotjhe weza maPhilo uDorh. Zweli Mkhize, bewamukelwa yiKhabinethi ngoVelabahlinze.  Nge-NHI, urhulumende unqophe ukuphumelelisa isibo pho sakhe somthethosisekelo sokuqalelela ipilo yomuntu woke ngokusezingeni eliphezulu, njengoba kusitjho isiGaba sama-27 somThethosisekelo kunye nemiNqopho YezeTuthuko EBambelelako YeHlangano YeenTjhaba EziBumbeneko.
I-NHI inqophe ukuthi woke amaSewula Afrika, abantu abahlalele safuthi, abaphalali, iimbotjhwa, amaphandle akhe thiweko nabo boke abentwana baqalelelwe ngesigaba sokuthoma sezamaphilo.  Amaziko wokuqalelela ngesigaba sokuthoma sezamaphilo anjengemitholapilo nabodorhodera azakuba lisango lokuthoma lokungena ehlelweni lokuqalelelwa ngezamaphilo. Kuzakuba simahla ukungena ezikweni lesizo lokuqalelelwa ngezamaphilo.
“Sekusikhathi eside khulu ihlelo lethu lokuqalelela ngezamaphilo lisebenza ngendlela engakalungi nengakasimeleli. Ihlelo lokuqalelela umphakathi ngezamaphilo  lithwele ingcenye ekulu yo mthwalo wezifo elizweni lekhethweli, njengoba liqalelela ama-84% wesitjhaba sekhethu nje, ukube kanti lineensetje nziswa ezincani kunehlelo langeqadi lokuqalelela zama philo, lona eliqalene noku siza nokwelapha i-16% kwaphe-la yesitjhaba seSewula Afrika,” kutjho uNgqongqotjhe uMkhize.  I-NHI inqophe ukulandela emagadangweni wamazwe anje nge-Britain ne-Japan, nawo esele asebenza ngamahlelo wawo wezepilo afana nayo i-NHI ye khethu le.
Isebenza Bunjani i-NHI?Ngesizo le-NHI, isigulani sizakutloliswa njengekhastama.
Amakhastama kuzakufuneka ukuthi aye emazikweni wokuqalelela ngesigaba sokuthoma sezamaphilo anjengemitholapilo namkha udorhodere, wona azakuba lisango lokuthoma ehlelweni lokuqalelelwa ngesizo lezamaphilo.
Isigulani sele sifikile ezikweni lokuqalelela ngesigaba sokuthoma sezamaphilo, udorhodere/isazi sezamaphilo uzakuqala athole ukuthi lesisigulani kufuze  sisizwe ngezinga liphi lokuqalelelwa ngezamaphilo, begodu nayibe kunesidingo, isigulaneso besidluliselwe kelinye iziko lo kwelapha kobanyana siyokuthola isizo lokwelatjhwa elingezelelweko.
Iingulani zizakuthola lelisizo lokwelatjhwa simahla, kodwana nayibe isigulani siya poro kudorhodere osipetjhalisti, isigulaneso sesizakubhadeliselwa ukwe latjhwa.
Kwenzekani-ke Ngamahlelo WeenKhwama ZokwElaphisa (amaMedical Aid Scheme)? Ngaphasi komthethomlingwa lo, amahlelo weenkhwama zokwe laphisa azakususwa kancani kancani – abe agcine aphelile njengomthombo osigaba soku thoma sokuqalelela ngezamaphilo.Lokhu-ke kutjho ukuthi iinkhwama zokwelaphisa zizakusebenza njengesingeze lelo sokwelaphisa esisekela i-NHI. Isingezelelo sokwelaphisa sizakwenza imisebenzi engekho ngaphasi kwe-NHI, njengokuhlinzelwa ukulungi sa isikhumba somzimba na mkha ukuhlinzelwa ukulu ngisa nanyana ngisiphi isitho somzimba esihlinzelwa ubuhle. “Woke amaSewula Afrika azakusizakala ngomsebenzi ozakwenziwa ngokuqalelela zamaphilo ngokusezingeni eliphezulu, ngaphandle koku bhadela,’ kuhlathulule uMkhize.

Ngomnyaka wee-2026 i-NHI izabe seyisebenza ngokupheleleko.Silusapho NyandaOmunye umma owasi nda ekankereni yesi beletho ukholwa uku thi abantu abasikazi kufuze banande bayokwenza i-pap smear emitholapilo yange makhabo.
UZibulani Dlamini (one minyaka ema-45)* uthi ukube wayenelwazi elingcono  ngekankere, wayezakuhlo lwa kusese nesikhathi.“Nayibe unande uba nee nhlabi engcenyeni yamathumbu esenzasi, kufuze uyozihlolisa.
Amanye amatshwayo kukopha nawusemsemeni namkha ngemva kokubuya emsemeni, ukuphuma kwetlelezi ngendlela engakafaneli, ukuya esikhathini ngendlela engakajayeleki, ukuzwa ubuhlungu obukhulu umuntu nakasesikhathini,” utjho ayelelisa nangokuthi kodwana kabanye abantu abasikazi akubi natshwayo nalinye; lokhu-ke ngikho kanye okwenza kube yinto eqakathekileko ukuthi umuntu anande azihlolisa.  Ngokwe-Cancer Association of South Africa (i-Cansa), ikankere yesibeletho yenzeka emaselini wengcenye yesibeletho  engenzasi ehlanganisa isibeletho nesitho sobufazi.
“Lomhlobo wekankere-ke ungowesibili emihlobeni evame khulu ebantwini abasikazi beSewula Afrika, kanti-ke ubangwa yingogwana ebizwa ngokuthi yi-papilloma yabantu, ekuyingogwana evamileko erhatjheka ngokuthintana ngesikhumba, nangamatlelezi womzimba kunye nangokuya emsemeni,” kuhlathulule i C a n s a  k u b u n z i n z o l w a z i  b a y o .
Ngokwe-Cansa, ukutholakala kwekankere yesibeletho  kusese nesikhathi kwandisa amathuba wokuyelapha  beyiphole ikankere le, begodu kungavimba nokuthoma  ukutjhuguluka kwamaseli wesibeletho abe yikankere.
Ikankere itholakele emzimbeni kaZibulani ngoSihlabantakana wee-2018 ngemva kokuya kwakhe emtholapilo aphethwe sihlabi engcenyeni yamathumbu engenzasi nephelvisini.
Uthi imiphumela ye-pap smear yakhe yabuya ngemva kwenyanga, wase uthunyelwa esibhedlela sangekhabo, sona esamdlulisela phambili esiBhedlela i-Nelson Mandela Academic eseMthatha, lapho wafika watjelwa khona ukuthi unekankere yesibeletho.
“Abodorhodere bamtjela ukuthi ngenca yokuthi ikankere eyayimphethe yasele inemirabhu esimelele khulu, ubungcono kwakukukuthi akhutjhwe isibeletho soke, ukwenzelela ukuthi ingasazokurhatjheka iye p h a m b i l i . ” UZibulani uthi kufuze ukuthi kuphunywe ijima lokufundisa abantu abasikazi khudlwana ngekankere yesibeletho, nokuthi ibalimaza njani abantu abasi kazi, nokuthi bangalifuna kuphi isizo lokwelatjhwa.
“Abantu abasikazi abasola inga kukhona okungakalungi kufuze bona bafune isizo lokwelatjhwa khonokho,” kutjho uZibulani aphetha isiyeleliso.
