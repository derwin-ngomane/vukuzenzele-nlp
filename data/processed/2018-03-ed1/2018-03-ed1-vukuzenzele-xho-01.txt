Ukubhalisela uvoto kuza kuba ngeyoKwi ndla yowama-2018

IKOmISh ONI YO NYuLO EzImELEYO (I-IEC) iqinisekisa okokuba abemi boMza ntsi Afrika ngangobuninzi babo babhalisela uvoto kunyulo lowama-2019.

More Matshediso

I-IEC yoMzantsi Afrika yazise umjikelo woku qala wempelaveki yoku bhalisela uvoto kunyulo lwama-2019, kwaye ibo ngoze abavoti abafanele kileyo kweli liphela ukuba batyelele izikhululo zabo zovoto bahlaziye iinkcukacha zabo kumqulu woluhlu lwabavoti okanye babhalisele uvoto.
IGosa eliyiNtloko loNyulo lekomishoni, uSy Mama bolo, uthe zonke izikhu lulo zovoto ezingama-22 612 ziza kuvula ngempela veki yomhla we-10  nowe-11 kweyoKwindla yowama-2018 ukususela ngentsimbi ye-8 kusasa ukuya kweye-5 emva kwemini. Oku kwenzelwa okokuba kube lula kwaye kuncede abavoti bahlaziye iinkcukacha zabo zovoto okanye ba bhalise. Okokuba ufudukele kwenye indawo engeyiyo le wawuhlala kuyo ukugqibela kwakho ukuvota, kufune ka uphinde ubhalise kwesi sithili sakho sitsha sovoto.  Abemi boMzantsi Afrika abaminyaka ili-16 nangaphezulu  nabanesazisi esi semthethweni okanye  ikhadi bangabhalisela uvoto. Okokuba  akubhalisanga, aku sayi kuvunyelwa okokuba uvote ngowama-2019.
Abavoti ababhalisileyo abaneenkcukacha zeedilesi zabo ezingekhoyo kumqulu woluhlu lwabavoti, iinkcu kacha ezifunekayo bangazi nikezela kwizikhululo zabo zovoto xa kubhaliswa.
“Sifuna abavoti beze baze basebenzise eli thuba,”  utshilo uMamabolo. “IKomi shoni yoNyulo kungoku nje ayikabinazo nangoku iidilesi zabavoti abamalunga nezigidi ezi-2.8. Ngaphezu koko kukho iidilesi ezizigidi ezi-3.5 ezihlelwe “njengezi ngagqibekanga” okanye “ezi ngacacanga.” Olu didi lufuna ukuphinda luhlahlelwe ukuqinisekisa okokuba ngaba zikho na ezinye iinkcukacha ezikhoyo,” wongeze ngeli tshoyo.
Ikomishoni ifumene iidile si ngokusebenzisa amali nge awohlukeneyo aquka ukuhlaziya iingxelo, iifayi li neendawo zokulondolo za izibhalo ezibalulekileyo. Oku ikwenzela ukukhangela nokufumana iidilesi ezinikezelwe ngabavoti kwimi nyaka engama-20 edluleyo; ngexesha iqokelela iidilesi ngexesha lonyulo loomasipala lowama-2016; nangokwe nza uphando lwangapha ndle ngaphambi kweempe laveki zokubhalisela unyulo lovalo-sikhewu.
Apho kuyimfuneko khona, abavoti ekufumaniseke okokuba babhaliswe kwizithi li zovoto ezingachanekanga, basuswa apho ngokweCa ndelo 12 (1)(d) loMthetho woNyulo.
“Siye safumana saze sasebenzisa, imithombo yeedi lesi ekwamanye  amacande lo ombuso afana neZiko lee Nkcukacha-manani loMza ntsi Afrika (i-Statistics South Africa), i-Arhente yeZibonelelo zezeNtlalo noMhla la-phantsi yoMzantsi Afrika (i-SASSA), u-Telkom, iPosi yoMzantsi Afrika neSebe leMicimbi yaseKhaya, apho sikwaziyo khona,  ” utshilo.

Sindisa imikhombe yeli

Amalinge oM zantsi Afrika asindise imikhombe emngciphekweni wokuphantse itshabalale kwiminyaka yooma-1960, kodwa isesemngciphekweni wokutshabalala njengoko sele isele ingama-20 000 kuphela

More Matshediso

uMphathiswa weSebe  lezeNdalo uEdna  Molewa, ukhuphe ingxelo yokuqala yenkqube la yowama-2018 malunga nendlela karhulumente edi-beneyo yecebo lolawulo ukuze kuliwe ukuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni.
Le ngxelo, equka ikota yokugqibela yowama-2017, ibonisa inkqubela ekuba njweni kwabantu abaliqela, uphando nempumelelo eku gwetyweni  kwabo bazinge la imikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni, nabathengisa  iimpondo zayo ngokungekho mthethweni nokuhla kanci nci kwenani lemikhombe ezingelwa ngokungekho mthethweni esizweni jike-lele kowama-2017 (1028), xa kuthelekiswa nowama-2016 (1054).
Ngowama-2017 kubanjwe iimpondo zemikhombe izihla ndlo ezisibhozo kwiSikhu lulo seNqwelo-moya seZi zwe-ngezizwe i-OR Tambo International Airport kwaza kwabanjwa abatyholwa abangama-502 ngokuzingela imikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni nabatyholwa ngokurhweba ngokungekho mthethweni abali-16. Lilonke inani lababanjwayo ngama-518, nani elo elihle livela kuma-680 kowama-2016.
“Ngelishwa, amagosa ethu angama-21 nawo abanjwa malunga nemicimbi enxulu mene nokuzingela imikho mbe ngokungekho mthethwe ni kowama-2017,” utshilo uMphathiswa Molewa.Ukuthintela ukuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni  Urhulumente namahlakani akhe basungule amanyathelo ngamanyathelo okuthintela ukuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni, kuquka iinkqubo zokubeka esweni ngokuhambahamba kwiipaka zesizwe nokuse benzisa ubuchwepheshe obukwiqondo eliphezulu.Phakathi kwenyanga  yoMqungu neyoMnga  yowama-2017 kuzingelwe imikhombe engama-504 ngokungekho mthethweni eKruger National Park (KNP). Loo nto yenze ukuba inani lihle ngeepesenti ezingama-24 xa kuthelekiswa nama-662 kowama-2016.“Ngenxa yecebo lethu lokulwa ukuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni e-KNP sibona lisihla inani lokuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokunge-kho mthethweni. Yimikhombe engama-2662 eyaziwayo ezingelwe ngokungekho mthethweni kowama-2017 xa kuthelekiswa nengama-2883 kowama-2016. Loo nto ithi oku kuzingelwa kuhle nge-7.6 yeepesenti,” uqinisekisile uMphathiswa.Kambe, inani lemikhombe ezingelwe ngokungekho mthethweni linyukile Kwazulu-Natal, eMntla Koloni, eMpumalanga, eFreyistata naseMntla Ntshona.
Ifuthe kuqoqoshoUkuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni nokurhweba ngezilwanyana zasendle okungekho mthe thweni kunefuthe elibi ekuphuhliseni uqoqosho lwelizwe nokudala imi sebenzi, kuquka noku phuhliswa koqoqosho lwezilwanyana nezityalo ezahlukeneyo okubandaka nya necandelo lezokhe nketho.“Ukuphela kwezinye zeentlo bo zezilwanyana zasendle eziyimbalasane eMzantsi Afrika ezifana nomkhombe, oyinxalenye ‘yeZona ziLwa nyana ziKhulu ziHlanu’ ezibizwa “i-Big Five,” kunga nefuthe elibi kwinani laba khenkethi abatyelela uMza ntsi Afrika. Uninzi lwabakhe nkethi lutyelela uMzantsi  Afrika kuba lufuna ukubona le Big Five ukutsho ke umkho mbe, ingonyama, inyathi, ihlosi nendlovu,” iSebe lezeNdalo litshilo kwingxelo eliyikhuphileyo.Ukuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni kubonwa njengento eyingozi kukhuseleko lwelizwe, nto leyo enganefuthe kwinani labakhenkethi abatyelela eli lizwe, kwaye urhulumente ukubhengeze njengolwaphulo-mthetho oluphambili esizweni.Izenzo ezingekho mthethweni ezihambelana nokuzingelwa kwemikhombe ngokungekho mthethweni nokurhweba ngeempondo zemikhombe okungekho mthethweni kunceda kuphela inani elincinci labophuli-mthetho.“La maqela abophuli-mthetho axhaphaza uku ntsonkotha kolu hlobo lolwa phulo-mthetho. Ngeli xesha le mibutho yemigulukudu ifumana ukuxhamla, urhulumente akakwazi ukuqinise kisa uphuhliso lwezentlalo noqoqosho kwaye ezi zenzo ziyondela ebantwini elizweni liphela,” iSebe liqinisekise latsho.
