Ukubhalisela ukuvota kuzoba  ngoNdasa wezi-2018

iKHomisHAni Yo KHetH o ezimele (i-ieC) iqinisekisa ukuthi labantu baseNi ngizimu Afrika abaningi ngendlela ngokungenzeka babhalisela ukuvota ngokhetho lowezi-2019.

More Matshedisoi

-IEC yaseNingizimu Afri ka seyimemezele impe lasonto yokuqala yokub halisela kwabavoti ukhetho lowezi-2019 futhi seyinxenxe abafanele ukuvota ezweni lonke ukuthi bavakashele izikhungo zabo zokuvota ukuze balungise imininingwane yabo ohlwini lwabavoti noma ukuze babhalisele ukuvota.
Isikhulu Sokhetho Esiyinhloko sekhomishana uSy Mamabolo uthe zonke izikhungo zokuvota eziyizi-22 612 zizovula ngempe-lasonto yangomhla ziyi-10 kanye namhla ziyi-11 kuNdasa wezi-2018, kusukela ngele-8 ekuseni kuze kube ngele-5 ntambama, ukuze kwenziwe kube lula futhi kusize abavoti ukuthi bafake imininingwane yabo yakamuva yokubhalisa noma ukuthi ba bhalise.
Uma uthuthele endaweni entsha kusukela mhla ugcina ukubha lisela ukuvota, kumele ubhalise esifundeni sakho sokuvota esisha.
Izakhamuzi zaseNingizimu Afrika ezineminyaka yobudala eyi-16 kanye nangaphezu-lu ezinomazisi noma ikhadi elisemthethweni zingabhalisela ukuvota. Uma ungabhalisanga, angeke uze uvunyelwe ukuthi uvote ngowezi-2019.
Abavoti ababhalisiwe, abanemininingwane yabo yekheli engekho ohlwini lwabavoti, bangahlinze-ka imininingwane edingekayo esikhungweni sabo sokuvota ngesikhathi sokubhalisa.
“Sidinga ukuthi abavoti bafike futhi basebenzise ithuba,” kusho uMamabolo.
“Ikhomishani Yokhetho Ezimele ayikabi namakheli abavoti cishe abayizigidi eziyi-2.8. ngaphezu kwalokho sinamakheli ayizigidi eziyi-3.5 abekwe kuhlu ‘lwa ngaphelele’ noma ‘angacacile’. Lo mkhakha usadinga ukuhlaziywa futhi ukuze kutholakale ukuthi ngabe kukhona yini eminye imininingwane etholakalayo,” kwengeza yena.
Ikhomishani ithola amakheli ngokusebenzisa izinhlelo ezahlu-kene, ezihlanganisa ukubukeza amarekhodi, amafayela kanye nezinqolobane zemibhalo uku-ze ibheke bese ifaka amakheli ahlinzekwe abavoti eminyakeni engama-20 edlule, ngokuqoqa amakheli ngokhetho lomasipala lowezi-2016, kanye nokwenza umsebenzi wokufuna bukhoma kungekafiki impelasonto yoku bhalisela ukhetho lokuchibiyela.

Sindisani obhejane bakithi

Imizamo yeNingizimu Afrika yabuyisa obhejane ekucisheni bashabalale ngeminyaka ye-1960, kodwa lolu hlobo lwezilwane luselokhu lusengozini njengoba zingama-20 000 kuphela ezikhona.

More Matshediso

UNgqongqoshe  Wezendawo, u-Ed na Molewa, usekhi phe umbiko wokuqala wezi-2018 wenqubekela phambili eseyibe khona ngokuphathelene nesu likahulumeni lokwengamela elididiyela izinhlaka eziningi lokuqeda ukuzingelwa kobhejane.
Umbiko, ohlanganisa nekota yokugcina yowezi-2017, ukhombisa inqubekela phambili ngokuphathelene nenani la-baboshiwe, uphenyo kanye nabazingela bese beshushu mbisa izimpondo zobhejane asebegwetshiwe kanye nokwe-hla kancane kwesibalo sobhe-jane abazingelwa kuzwelonke ngowezi-2017 (1028), uma kuqhathaniswa nowezi-2016 (1054).
Ngowezi-2017 kwaba khona ukwemukwa kwezimpondo zobhejane okuyisishiyagalo mbili Esikhumulweni Sezindiza i-OR Tambo International futhi kwaboshwa abangama-502 abasolwa ngokuzingela obhe jane nabayi-16 abasolwa ngo kushushumbisa izimpondo zobhejane, nokwaholela eku-theni isibalo sibe ngama-518, sehla sisuka kuma-680 ngowe-zi-2016.
“Kuyadumaza ukuthi, abasebenzi bethu abangama-21 baboshelwa ukuzibandaka nya nokuzingelwa kobhejane ngowezi-2017,” kusho uNgqongqoshe uMolewa.
Ukunqanda ukuzingelwa kobhejaneUhulumeni kanye nabambi sene nabo sebesebenzise izi ndlela ezahlukene nokunqanda ukuzingelwa kobhejane, ezi hlanganisa izinhlelo zokuqapha eziqiwini zikazwelonke kanye nokusebenzisa ubuchwe pheshe obuguqula isimo.
Phakathi kukaMasingana kanye noZibandlela wezi-2017, kwazingelwa obhejane abanga-ma-504 e-Kruger National Park (KNP), nokungamaphesenti ehle ngama-24 kunama-662 angowezi-2016.
“Ngenxa yendlela yokunqanda ukuzingelwa kobhejane e-KNP sibona yehla imizamo yokuzingelwa kobhejane esiqiwini, kuqoshwe izi-2662 ngowezi-2017 uma kuqhatha-niswa nezi-2883 ngowezi-2016. Lokhu kukhombisa ukwehla kwamaphesenti ngo-7.6,” ku-qinisa uNgqongqoshe.
Noma kunjalo, sekwenyuke isibalo sobhejane abazingelwe KwaZulu-Natali, eNyakatho Kapa, eMpumalanga, eFreyi stata kanye nasesiFundazweni saseNyakatho-Ntshonalanga.
Umthelela kwezomnothoUkuzingelwa kobhejane kanye nokushushumbiswa kwezi lwane kunomthelela omubi ekuthuthukisweni komnotho wezwe kanye nasekudalweni kwamathuba omsebenzi, oku-hlanganisa ukuthuthukiswa komnotho onhlobonhlobo wezemvelo ohlanganisa no mkhakha wezokuvakasha.
“Ukulahleka kohlobo lwezi lwane oluyingqalabutho kakhulu lwaseNingizimu Afrika olufana nobhejane, nongesinye ‘Sezilwane Ezinku-lu Ezinhlanu’ kungaba nomthe-lela omubi ngokuphathelene nenani lezivakashi eNingizimu Afrika. Izivakashi eziningi eziza eNingizimu Afrika zizela ukuzobona Izilwane Ezinhlanu Ezinkulu, nokuwubhejane, ibhubesi, inyathi, ingwe kanye nendlovu,” kusho isitatimende soMnyango Wezendawo.
Ukuzingelwa kobhejane sekwaziswe njengengozi yezokuphepha ezweni, enga ba nomthelela kuamanani ezivakashi ezivakashela izwe, futhi uhulumeni usememezele ukuthi kuwubugebengu obuse qhulwini kuzwelonke.
Izenzo ezingekho semthe thweni ezihlanganiswa  nokuzingelwa kobhejane kanye nokushushumbiswa kwezi mpondo zobhejane kuhlo mulisa amaqembu amancane emigulukudu.
“La maqembu emigulukudu ahlomula ngenxa yokuba yinkimbinkimbi kwalolu hlo-bo lobugebengu. Ngesikhathi amaqembu emigulukudu ehlo-mula, kuthikamezeka ukukwa-zi kukahulumeni ukuqinisekisa kwezenhlalo kanye nomnotho ngenxa yokuthi amaqembu obugebengu obuhleliwe kanye nokusebenza kwawo kuba mandla emiphakathini esezweni lonke,” kuqinisekisa uMnyango.
