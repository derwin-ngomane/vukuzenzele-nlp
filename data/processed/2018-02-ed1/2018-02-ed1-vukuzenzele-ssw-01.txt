Tinswane letisandza kubelekwa tihlaselwa yiListeriosis  kalula

BaNTfu BaSENIN gIzIMu afrIKa  kufanele bacinisekise kuhla nteka kwekudla kwabo njengobe sekuvele iListeriosis.

Litiko leTemphilo likubuke ngeliso lelibucayi kuvela kwe-Listeriosis. Bali-119 bantfu lasebabikwe ngekutsi bahlaselwa ngule sifo kusukela ngamhla ti-5 Ingongoni. Uma babonkhe bangema-727 bantfu labacinisekisiwe kutsi bahlaselwe ngulesifo ngemnyaka wa-2017.
Sikhungo Sekuvikela Nekulwa Netifo sichaza i-Listeriosis  njengesifo lesiyingoti kodvwa lesi laphelako futsi lesivikele-kako esibangwa ligciwane i-Listeria monocytogenes.  Sitfolakala emhlabatsini, emantini nasetibhidvweni. Tintfo letentiwa ngetilwa nyana  – njengenyama nelubisi , kudla lokutfolakala elwandle kanye nekudla lokufana netitselo netibhi dvo letingenaso lesifo.
Nanome i-Listeriosis ihlasela wonkhe umu ntfu, bantfu labasengotini lenkhulu yekuhlaselwa ngulesifo ngemaswane la-sandza kubelekwa, bantfu labagugile, bantfu labasika-ti labakhulelwe nebantfu labanemasotja emtimba laphansi njengebantu la-bane-HIV , sifo sashukela, mdlavuta, sifo sesibindzi nobe sifo setinso lesinga lapheki.
Bantfu labahlaseleka lula ngalesifo ngemaswane – lanemalanga lange-28 ate lwe, nebantfu labane minyaka lenge-15 kuya ku-49. Labantfu banaleminyaka benta 70% webantfu labahlaselwa ngulesifo.
Litiko ligcugcutele bomake labakhulelwe kutsi babhalise kuMomConnect kute batewukwatiswa kabanti ngeListeriosis njengobe bantfwana la banemalanga langephansi kwalange-28 bahlaseleka kakhulu.
“Uma sibuka lubalobalo lwebantfu labatsintseka ko, sicaphele kutsi kubo bonkhe labantfu bahlaseleka lula, ngemaswane lahlaseleka kakhulu. Uma sibukisisa ngekwemnyaka sukela ekubelekweni kuya kilabo babeminyaka lenge-93 budzala, ema swane wodvwa enta 40% webantfu labahlaseleka lula,” kusho Indvuna Yetemphilo Dk Aaron Motsoaledi.  Indvuna icele tisebenti tetemphilo kutsi tica phele kakhulu maticilonga bomake labakhulelwe.
“Kukhishwe sicelo lesikhetsekile kutisebe nti tetemphilo nebantfu bonkhe kutsi banakekele bonkhe labasikati la bakhulelwe ngalokukhe tsekile. Umangabe use benta ngemntfu lokhule-lwe nobe luswane hlolisisa kutsi ngabe lowo muntfu akanaso lesifo,” ushilo.

Babuyiselwa sitfunti labo labasindze ekuhlaselweni ngetemacansi 

BaNTfu LaBaSINDzE ekuhlukunyetweni batfola luncedvo kumatiko ekunakekela.

luthando Motswai

Litiko leTemphilo  lase-Gauteng litsetse tinyatselo tekuncedza bantfu labatisulu tekuhlukunyetwa ngetemacansi nange kwebulili ngekuvula iSi nakekelwe Thuthuzela Care Centre esibhedlela iThelle Mogoerane eVosloorus.
Lesikhungo sivulelwe ku sita bantfu labatisulu teku hlukunyetwa ngetemacansi nekubanika lusito lolulungilo lwetekunakekelwa, kwelule kwa, nekufinyelela kubaseshi nebashushisi labasebenta ngekutikhandla ndzawonye.  Sikhulumi seLitiko Lese mang Matuka utsite labatisulu ekucaleni bebahamba maba nga lamadze kute bafinyelele kuletinsita. Lesikhungo lesisandza kuvulwa sitawusita bantfu baseKatlehong, eThokoza, eVosloorus naseti ndzaweni letisedvute.
“NjengeLitiko, sifuna kutsi kubekhona tinsita letincono temphilo kumntfu wonkhe ngekucecesha nekufundzisa tisebenti temphilo ngema khono lakhetsekile ekuncedza tisulu teludlame. Kuleminyaka lemitsatfu leyendlule, tise benti tekwelapha letinge-106 tiphumelele etifundvweni te-Sexual Assault Care Practitioner Course.”Utsite litiko litawuchubeka lisebentisane netinhlanga no letingekho ngaphansi kwahulumende (ema-NGO) nalabanye labancedzisa ngekwesekela ngetengco ndvo ngekugcizelela ku gwema kakhulu umonakalo lowenteka ngemva kwekuhlukumeteka.
EmaThuthuzela Care Centre asungulelwe kwenta ncono  indlela emacala ekudlwengu lwa nalamanye ekuhlukunye twa ngetemacansi atsatfwa ngayo. Labatisulu nyalo bance dvwa ngendlela lenesitfunti nasendzaweni lenakekelako.
Letikhungo tingephansi kweMkhandlu Wetebushu shisi Wavelonkhe (i-NPA), kanye neLitiko Letemphilo, Litiko Letekutfutfukiswa Kwetenhlalakahle neLitiko Letebulungiswa Nekutfu tfukiswa Kwemtsetfosisekelo, kanye neMbutfo Wetemaphoyisa eNingizimu Afrika (i-SAPS).
Kunetikhungo letisikhombi sa eGauteng. Sikhungo se kucala savulwa esibhedlela iChris Hani Baragwanath Hospital ngemnyaka wa-2000.
Dk Efadzwa Tipoy, umphatsi lomkhulu wekwelapha lo phetse iSinakekelwe  Thuthuzela Care Centre, weluleke bantfu kutsi umangabe bahle babetisulu tekuhlukunyetwa ngetemacasi, kufanele kutsi bayewubika kulesikhu-ngo ngekuphutfuma.
“Letikhungo tinika bantfu umutsi wekuvikela kutfola sifo semacansi kufaka ekhatsi kuvuvukala kwesibi ndzi neligciwane lesandvulelangculazi (i-HIV).  Lona lohlukumetekile utawukiketwa emaphilisi ekuvikela kukhulelwa. Ungagezi. Wota naleti mphahla lobe utigcokile kulesikhungo utifake ephepheni hhayi eplastikini,” kusho Dk Tipoy.
Wengete ngekutsi kusukela kwavulwa lesikhungo, linyukile lizinga lebantfu labasebentisa letinsita lete tfulwako kantsi futsi se kunesitfunti ngobe nyalo bantfu sebayakhona kugeza umabacedza kuhlolwa.
Ingcinamba leyodvwa lo mtfolamphilo lesolo  ubukene nayo kutsi ba ntfu bafika sebendlulelwe sikhatsi – kubalulekile kutsi bufakazi bugcogcwe nesisu lu sinikwe umutsi ngembi kwekutsi kuphele ema-awa lange-72 sihlaselwe.
