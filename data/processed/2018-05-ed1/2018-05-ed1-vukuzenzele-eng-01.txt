Land claim reaps rewards

A KWAZULU-NATAL (KZN) community will soon be enjoying the rewards of a successful land claim that they have turned into a private game reserve

Hlengiwe Ngobese


The first blackowned private game reserve is on the cards for KZN, thanks to a R15 million government investment to the Mayibuye Community Trust. The word mayibuye, which means to return in isiZulu, is also the name of the 5 581-hectare Mayibuye Game Reserve, which is located between Pietermaritzburg and Durban.
