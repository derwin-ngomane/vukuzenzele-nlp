Vuk’uzenzeleIZIQHAMO ZENKULULEKO Urhulumente uzi misele ukuxhasa uPhuhliso lwaBa ntwana abaseLula (i-ECD), into leyo eyaziwa lihlabathi liphela njengelona phulo libalulekileyo kulo naliphi na ilizwe kuphuhliso lweza khono zabantu.  "Ukuba sifuna ukulwa i ndlala, kufuneka sifundise abantwana abaphuma ku makhaya asokolayo," watsho uMongameli Cyril Ramapho sa ngethuba esenza iNtetho engoBume beSizwe (i-SoNA) yakhe yowama-2018.
Ukungafumani mathuba aneleyo noncedo ngethuba umntwana eselula kungabe nzela ubunzima abantwana abasakhulayo ngolo hlobo abe mancinci namathuba abo okuphumelela ebomini.
Nangona kunjalo, ngowa ma-2009 ingcali ye-ECD yeDyunivesiti yaseMntla Ntshona, uGq Juliana Se leti ngethuba kubanjwe iNdibano yoCweyo yeBha nki yeHlabathi engePhu lo loNakekelo noPhuhli so lwaBantwana baseLula e-Afrika wathi urhulumente wocalucalulo wayengaba niki mathuba nancedo nabo abantwana.  La rhulumente wayenge namigaqo-nkqubo ye-ECD, nditsho neyabantwana aba phuma kumakhaya afumi leyo. Iinkonzo ze-ECD  ezazikhona zazikwindawo ezithile futhi zazixhanyulwa ligcuntswana labantwana.  UGq Seleti uthi imi gaqo-nkqubo ecacileyo  yaqaliswa ukuqulunqwa ekuqaleni kweminyaka  yowama-1990, emva kokuba lowo wayesakuba nguMo ngameli uNelson Mandela ekhululwe entolongweni.
Kwathi kufika unyaka wama-2003 iSebe loPhuhli so loLuntu, eleMfundo esi Siseko kunye nelezeMpilo abe sele enayo eyawo imi gaqo-nkqubo ye-ECD; ena macandelo namacandelwana futhi i-ECD yabekelwa imali yayo bucala kuhlahlo lwabi wo-mali ngonyaka wama2000.  I-ECD ukusukela ngoko yaba yezinye zeenkqubo urhulumente azikhokelise phambili yaze neKhabhi nethi yaphumeza uMgaqonkqubo weSizwe we-ECD eHlangeneyo ngeyoMnga ngowama-2015, ukuze ku guqulwe iindlela iinkonzo ze-ECD ezihanjiswa ngayo eMzantsi Afrika ngokulu ngisa iindawo ezibonakali sa ukusilela ngasemva  nokuqinisekisa ukuba zi yafumenaka iinkonzo zeECD ezibanzi, ezixha nyulwa ngumntu wonke ne zilinganayo. Iinkonzo ze-ECD zisiwa phantsi kweSebe leMfundo esiSisekoAmaziko e-ECD okanye izikolo-mpeleso zisusiwe kwiSebe loPhuhliso loLu ntu ngoku ziphantsi kweSebe leMfundo esiSiseko, futhi sithetha nje abantwana aba ngaphezulu kwama-700 000 bafunda kwizikolo-mpeleso kulo lonke eli.
"Eli linyathelo elibaluleke kakhulu kumaphulo emfu ndo yabantwana abanci nci kweli lizwe futhi, ukuba liqhutywe ngendlela efane lekileyo, liza kunceda kakhu lu kumalinge okuzalisekisa umbono wesiCwangciso seSi zwe soPhuhliso (i-NDP) so wama-2030 . "Kodwa ukuba azikho izicwangciso ezica cileyo nezibambekayo ze ndlela eliza kuqaliswa ngayo eli phulo, akukho nto liza kuyixhamla eli lizwe okanye abantwana balo," utshilo uMongameli Ramaphosa.
Inyathelo elilandelayo ngo ku kukuba kumiselwe ixesha eliyiminyaka emibini enya nzelekileyo yokuba wonke umntwana afunde ekritshi okanye kwisikolo-mpeleso, ukulungisela iminyaka e nzima yeBanga 1, njengoko wayetshilo uMongameli Ramaphosa kwiSoNA yakhe yowama-2019.
"Omnye umba obaluleke kakhulu kukuphucula nga mandla izakhono zokufu nda ngokuqonda zabantwa na abasaqala isikolo. Oku kubalulekile kumalinge okuxhobisa abantwana  ngezakhono ukuze bakwa zi ukuqhuba kakuhle kwi zifundo zabo nasebomini kwaye ubungqina obuninzi buyatsho ukuba imfundo sesona sixhobo sibaluleki -leyo kwidabi lokulwa indla la, intswelo-misebenzi noku ngalingani," utshilo uMo ngameli.
"Siza kuqinisekisa ukuba siyazongeza izixhobo zoku funda zabantwana abase lula abafunda kwisigaba esisiseko futhi kule minya ka mithandathu izayo siza kunika umntwana nga mnye kuzo zonke izikolo eMzantsi Afrika iincwadi zokusebenzela nezokufu nda kwitablet . Siza kuqala ngezo zikolo zazihlelekile ngaphambili nezikwindawo zabantu abangathathi ntwe ni, kuquka nezikolo ezina mabanga nezigaba ezahlu keneyo, ezisezifama nezase -maphandleni," wongeze watsho.
UMphathiswa weMfudo esiSiseko u-Angie Motshe kga uthi isebe likulungele ukuqalisa ngeencwadi zoku funda ezi-elekthronikhi kulo nyaka uzayo.
NgeyoMnga kowama-2019 iSebe leMfundo esiSiseko lisebenzisana neVodacom Foundation, iNelson Mande la Foundation iQumrhu leza baseTyhini leZizwe ezi Manyeneyo (i-UN) kunye neGlobal Citizen zinikezele ngeZiko le-ECD eDivhani Creche naseFrank Ravele School of Excellence eVhe mbe, eLimpopo.
Le kritshi, engamanye wamaziko ee-ECD ali-15 akhethiwe aza kulungiswa yona iqalwe  phantsi uku lingiswa yaze yafakelwa  izinto ezintsha.
