Vuk’uzenzeleIsiqalo esitsha eMzantsi Afrika INkqubo yeYES liqalelo likaMongameli uRamaphosa elilisango lokuba ulutsha lufumane imisebenzi.Dale HesSekuphele unyaka omnye ncam, emva kokuba uMongame li uCyril Ramaphosa eqa lise isiqalo esitsha eMzant si Afrika. Kwixesha nje eli futshane emva kokubamba isikhundla sakhe, uMonga meli wenze iNtetho kaMo ngameli engoBume beSizwe ethabathekisayo nevusele le ithemba, imincili nente mbeko kwizigidi zabantu ababebukele kulo lonke ili zwe.
Kwabakho imincili enzu lu engqonge iNtetho kaMo ngameli engoBume beSi zwe (iSoNA) yowama-2018.
Bekufanelekile ukuba le ntetho yenziwe ngoNyaka wesiKhumbuzo seMinya ka eliKhulu kaNelson Ma ndela: ukususela kwisiqalo solawulo lwentando yesi ninzi akazange umongameli waseMzantsi Afrika abuve ngolu hlobo ubunzima bomthwalo wokulindele ke kuye obekwe emagxeni wakhe. Kodwa uMongameli uRamaphosa uwawole onke amanqaku kuluntu lwase Mzantsi Afrika. Le ntetho ichukumise kwimibandela eyiyona itshisayo elijongene nayo eli lizwe; yenze izi ndululo zezisombululo ezinobulumko; yaze ya bonakalisa ukuzinikela ngokwenene ekwakhiweni koMzantsi Afrika ongcono.
 Imiba yogqaliselo lweSONA yowama-2018Ngokufanayo nezinye iiSoNA ezininzi zangaphambili, imiba yokudalwa kwemisebenzi, uhlumo lwezoqoqosho, ukulwa indlala, ezemfundo, inkathalelo yezempilo, uhle ngahlengiso  lwezemihlaba, umakulingwane ngokwesini nokulwa ulwaphulo-mthetho kunye norhwaphilizo ibekwe phambili kwi-ajenda yeSoNA yowama-2018.
UMongameli uRamaphosa uthe, nangona kunjalo, eli lelinye lamathuba okuzalwa ngokutsha nangokupheleleyo koMzantsi Afrika. Thuba elo ekuya kufuneka kulo ukuba sibe kwinqanaba elitsha lo kwenza izisombululo. “Sinikwe uxanduva  lokwakha isizwe esitsha, siqubisana nezenzo ezinge nabulungisa zexesha elidlu lileyo nezangoku zokungali ngani. Siyabongozwa ukuba senze njalo phantsi kweemeko ezinzima.”Ukudalwa kwemisebenziUndoqo ekulweni indlala, kukudalwa kwemisebenzi okungomnye wemiba ese kuqaleni ephakanyiswe ngu Ramaphosa kwintetho yakhe.
“Esizikithini se-ajenda yethu yesizwe yowama-2018 kukudalwa kwemisebenzi, ingakumbi eyolutsha,” utshilo uRamaphosa.
Ngokunyuka kwentswe lo-ngqesho eMzantsi Afrika, uMongameli ugxininise isidingo esingxamisekileyo sokudalwa kwemisebenzi ngokuthathwa kwamanya thelo aliqela. Isibhengezo seMbizo yemiSebenzi kaMo ngameli, ebandakanya iingxo xo phakathi kwecandelo lika rhulumente, elezoshishino, noluntu, samkelwe ngezandla ezishushu.
Le Mbizo yenzeke ekuqaleni kweyeDwarha, ibonakele ixabisekile ekudaleni u mhlahla-ndlela wokudalwa kwemisebenzi. Isivume lwano seNkqubo-sikhokelo esityikitywe kule mbizo sichaze ngokufutshane ii ndlela ezingaphezu kwama-70 zongenelelo ekugxilwe kuzo, eziya kuthi phakathi kwezinye izinto, zomeleze izinyanzeli so zalapha ekhaya, zandise ziphinde zinabise urhwebo lokuthumela iimveliso ku mazwe angaphandle, zidale imijelo yokufaka ulutsha emisebenzini nokuphuhlisa amacandelo afana nelezolimo elokwenza iimveliso, eleze migodi nelezoqoqosho olwe nziwe ngemveliso ephuma kwenye imveliso.
Ngenxa yolutsha olucha tshazelwe kakhulu yintswe lo-ngqesho, uMongameli uRamaphosa uzibeke phambili kwangoko iinkqubo ezinoku lunceda. INkonzo yeNgqesho yoLutsha (i-YES) yasungulwa kwiinyanga ezimbini emva kweSoNA yowama-2018, kwaye inika amashishini indlela entsha yokuqesha ul utsha ngokuluhlawula ngeli xesha lusafunda amava omse benzi. Kuthe kuphela unyaka wama-2018, zabe iinkampani ezingama-266 sele zityikityile kwinkqubo yeYES , kanti bangama-5,500 abantu abatsha  asebethathiwe emisebenzini.
Ukukhulisa uqoqoshoUqoqosho lwaseMzantsi  Afrika beluhluma ngokuco tha, lwade lwangena nakwi meko yokudodobala ekeqaleni kowama-2018. Ukuthenjwa ngabatyali-mali bekusoloko kuphantsi, kanti amacande lo anesakhono esiphezulu ebengayifumani inkxaso ayi dingayo ukuze abe negalelo kuqoqosho.
Ngokuhambelana nentetho emfutshane yakhe yesiqalo esitsha, uMongameli uRama phosa uliqalise kwangoko iphulo lotyalo-mali. Utyumbe abathunywa bombuso bohlo bo olulodwa abane ukuba bahambe naye kwilinge lokuya kuzibika ukuze batsale utyalo-mali oluyitriliyoni enye nezigidi ezibini zeerandi (R1.2 seetriliyoni) ngowama-2023.
UMongameli uRamaphosa uye wakhenketha nehlabathi jikelele ukuze ‘abuyisele intembeko yamazwe anga phandle' ukuba atyale imali eMzantsi Afrika.
Eli phulo liqukunjelwe ngeNgqungquthela yoTya lo-mali yaseMzantsi Afrika ngeyeDwarha, apho uMon gameli ebhengeze khona ukuba utyalo-mali olunga ngeebhiliyoni ezingamakhulu amabini anesithoba eerandi (R290 eebhiliyoni) luqinise kisiwe, luze ludityaniswe neebhiliyoni ezingamakhulu mane eerandi (R400 eebhiliyo ni) ezisisithembiso samazwe ayetyelelwe ngethuba lephulo lotyalo-mali. Kucingeleka ukuba olu tyalo-mali xa lu dibene luza kongeza iibhiliyo ni ezingamakhulu amathathu anamashumi amathathu (R330 eebhiliyoni) kwisambuku semveliso yangaphakathi (i-GDP) yoMzantsi Afrika ngowama-2024, kuze kuda leke imisebenzi engafikelela kuma-275 000 ngonyaka.
Uvuselelo loqoqosho  lukaMongameli uRama phosa kunye nesicwangciso sokubuyiselwa ngokutsha koqoqosho sichongwe nje ngolunye ungenelelo olu ngundoqo lokoyisa imiqo bo esendleleni yoqoqosho lwaseMzantsi Afrika. Ngoku bhengezwe kweyoMsintsi, umbumbutho wezithembiso  uyikhawulezisile inkcitho ye ziseko ezingundoqo, uhlaziyo lwezonxibelelwano lomnxeba nakumacandelo ezemigodi, ukuqhutywa kweeprojekthi ngokudibeneyo zikarhulu mente  nezabezoshishino kunye nenkqubo engena bugocigoci yokufunyanwa kweevisa eyakwenza kube lula kwiinkampani zezi zwe-ngezizwe ukuba zenze ushishino eMzantsi Afrika.
Ukusiphula urhwaphilizo neengcambu nokomeleza inkqubo yolawuloUMongameli ubonakalise ukuzinikela ngenene eku lusiphuleni neengcambu urhwaphilizo phakathi kuka rhulumentehe nakumashishi ni karhulumente.  Ikomishoni yophando lokuthinjwa ko mbuso iqalise kwangoko emva kokungena esikhundleni sakhe, njengoko kube njalo nangeekomishoni zophando e-Eskom naseSARS.
Utshintsho kwizikhundla zeenkokeli kumashishini ka rhulumente sesinye isalathiso sokuzimisela kukaMonga meli sokulwa urhwaphilizo nokomeleza inkqubo yolawu lo.
