Distributed by: Printed by:NGEKUSUNGULWA kwee-Recruitment  (kufakwa  kweticelo temsebenti ku-inthanethi), hulumende ukhombise kutinikela kwakhe ekutfutfukiseni timphilo telusha nalabo lesebaphotfulile etikhungweni tetemfundvo lephakeme.
Labafuna umsebenti sebatawukhona kufaka ticelo  temisebenti yahulumende ngekusebentisa luhlelo lwe e-Recruitment  lolwasungu lwa yiNdvuna yeLitiko Le temisebenti Yahulumende Nekuphatsa Ayanda Dlodlo.
LeNdvuna itsite loluhlelo lwee-Recruitment  seluca lile kusebenta kuleLi tiko  Letemisebenti Ya hulumende Nekuphatsa  (i-DPSA) kantsi lutawuca la kusebenta kuwo onkhe ematiko ahulumende ku ngasikudzala.
“Loluhlelo lwee-Recrui tment  luyincenye yenchubo  lechutjwa kuye wonkhe hulumende leyenta kube lula kutsi bantfu, ikakhu lukati lusha, lufake ticelo temisebenti kumatiko ahu lumende.  “Lenchubo itawufaka ekhatsi kubuyeketwa kwemitsetfo lemisiwe kuto tonkhe tikhundla letipha nsi kuhulumende kute labo labafaka ticelo tetikhundla letiphansi labangenalo lwa ti lwemsebenti bakhone ku ngena kuletikhala letipha nsi,” kusho Ndvuna Dlodlo.  Utsite abakuphiki kweku tsi loluhlelo lolusetjentiswa nyalo lunetihibe kulabo labafuna imisebenti ka ntsi ematiko ahulumende awakasuki noma awakacali kuhambisana naletingucu -ko tetheknoloji letikhona naletindlondlobalako.
“Kulabafuna umsebenti, loluhlelo lolusha lwee-Re cruitment lutawuba yinzuzo ngoba lusebentisa i-intha nethi kantsi luyafinyele leka kunoma nguyiphi indzawo lapho lona lofuna umsebenti atfolakala khona ngoba sicelo sakhe usifaka endzaweni yinye lapho ku fakwa khona ticelo,” asho ebeka njalo.  Indvuna itsi loluhlelo lutawenta kutsi labafaka ticelo bakhone kufaka ema-CV nemadokhumenti abo lesekelako ngesikhatsi sinye kantsi futsi lenchubo itabenta bonge kulokwe nta emakhophi kute bafake ticelo letihlukene.  “Kubalulekile kugcizelela kutsi ticelo temisebenti kuHulumende titawuchu beka titsatfwe ngalendlela letayelekile yekugcwalisa lifomu iZ83  kute kusi takale labo labangenato tinsita tekufinyelela ku-i nthanethi kantsi litawubuye litfolakale ngendlela yedijithali ku-inthanethi kwentela indlela leshesha ko nalelula yekufaka sicelo semsebenti kulabo labane-inthanethi,” washo njalo.  Indvuna itsite inzuzo yematiko ahulumende kutsi loluhlelo lubavumela kwekutsi bakhangise tikha la temisebenti ngekwe-ele kthronikhi kantsi futsi kwehlisa tindleko taleti ndlela letisetjentiswa nyalo tekukhangisa kumaphe phandzaba.  Lenchubo lensha yentfu tfuko ingumtamo weluba njiswano emkhatsini weLi tiko Letemisebenti Yahulu mende Nekuphatsa kanye Nelitiko lee-Government laseGauteng.
Lelitiko lee-Governme nt laseGauteng liniketa letinsita letidzingekako tekusebentisa loluhlelo, i-Ejensi  Yelwatiso Lwethe knoloji yeMbuso (i-SITA) yona isebentisana nalo ngekuniketa emakhono etetheknikhi nekutfutfuki swa kwetakhiwonchanti.
