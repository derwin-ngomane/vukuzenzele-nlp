Distributed by: Printed by:NGOKUHLONYWA KWE  e-Recruitment , urhulumende utjengise ukuzibophelela kwakhe ngokwenza ngcono amaphilo welutjha neweemfundiswa.
Abamakethako  bazokukghona  ukwenza iimbawo zemisebenzi yeembusweni ngehlelo le e-Recruitment  elihlonywe nguNgqongqo tjhe wezabaSebenzi baka Rhulumende nokuPhatha (i-DPSA) u-Ayanda Dlodlo.
UNgqongqotjhe uthe ihle lo lee-Recruitment  lingeni swa ngeengaba kuthonywe nge-DPSA bese lizokusa tjalaliswa kiyo yoke imi nyango yombuso. “Ihlelo le e-Recruitment  liyingcenye yomsebenzi karhulumende wokwenzela abantu ubu lula bokufaka iimbawo zemisebenzi yembusweni, khulukhulu ilutjha.
“Ikambiso le izoku faka hlangana godu nokubuyekezwa kweka mbiso nemithetho yokuza liswa kweenkhala zezin ga eliphasi embusweni ngomnqopho wokunikela ithuba abasathomako na batlhoga ilemuko,” Kwatjho  uNgqongqotjhe uDlodlo.
Wathi angeze kwaphikwa bonyana ihlelo lokuqa tjha lagadesi lisitjhijilo kabamakethako begodu iminyango yombuso ayi kabukusikinyeka nanyana itjhugulukele kuthekhnoloji ethuthukileko.
Wathi, “Kilabo abama kethako, ihlelo elitjheli i-e-Recruitment  lizokuba lisizo ngombana lisebenza nge-inthanethi begodu lifikeleleka kiyo yoke imi khakha nabamakethako bakghona ukuthumela iimbawo zabo kubuthelelo mininingwana linye”.
UNgqongqotjhe uthe lelihlelo lizokusiza abenzii mbawo ngokuthumela amaCV wabo nemitlolo yeziqu zabo esekelako kanye be godu ihlelweli lizokusiza ngokonga ekugayweni kwamaphepha amanengi.
Wathi, “Kuqakathekile ukugandelela bonyana iimbawo zemiSebenzi ye mBusweni zisazokwamu kelwa nazithunyelwa ngefo romo i -Z83 ukuqalelela labo abangakghoni ukufikelela i-inthanethi kodwana i-intha nethi izokusebenzisa ihlelo ledijithali ukwenza kube lula ukuthumela iimbawo kilabo abane-inthanethi”.
UNgqongqotjhe uthe leli hlelo lenzela urhulumende ubulula bokukhangisa iinkhala nge-inthanethi be godu ngalokho kuzakwehla iindleko ezibangwa yika mbiso yagadesi yokukha ngisa kumaphephandaba.
Umzamosisungulo ono buhlakani lo umamano wokubambisana hlangana kwe-DPSA nomNyango we Gauteng wee-Government .
UmNyango we-Gauteng we-e-Government  unikela ngeensetjenziswa ezitlho gekako zokusebenzisa leli hlelo, kanti i-Ejensi yeThe khnoloji yeLwazi lomBuso (i-SITA) isiza ngokunikela ngamakghonofundwa wesi terhiniki newokwenza ngco no umthangalasisekelo.
